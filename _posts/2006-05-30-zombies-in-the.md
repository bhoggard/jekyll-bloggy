<p>Sixspace in Los Angeles just opened a show of oil on canvas and oil on paper works by Chad Robertson, titled <em>Rise</em>.  They are inspired by</p>
<blockquote>
<p>&#8230; the films of George A. Romero and the zombie genre. For decades these films, in particular the Romero films, have explored current social or political issues and events that are channeled through this idea of a mindless, yet human, animal &#8211; a zombie.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Here are some images.  I imagine these are difficult to photograph.  The ghostly technique is impressive, given the medium.  Also, hairy-chested zombies / zombie victims are <span class="caps">HOT</span>.</p>
