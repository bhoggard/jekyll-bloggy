<p>One of our great discoveries in Miami, thanks to our host Michael, was the Gallery House project.  It&#8217;s an early 20th century house filled to the brim with art.  There are things hanging on the walls, in the closets, and there are wall paintings and installations too.  The vibe was more like visiting a show in someone&#8217;s loft in the East Village a decade or more ago than being in Miami for the whole art fair thing.</p>
<p>Not only did we enjoy the work, as we were leaving we saw two white possums sitting in the tree in the front yard, watching the activities.  They were in a tree to the right of this view:</p>
<p><img alt="GalleryHouse-miami.jpg" src="/mt/archives/GalleryHouse-miami.jpg" width="500" height="334" /></p>
