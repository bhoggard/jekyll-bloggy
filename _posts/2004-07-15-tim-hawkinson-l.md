<p>They&#8217;re changing <a href="http://www.whiteboxny.org/program/sixfeet04.html">the shows</a> (a combination of a work on the video monitor outside plus something in the window) every week, so go now to see the <a href="http://www.whiteboxny.org/program/sixfeet04/weektwo.html">excellent one up right now</a>, curated by Lawrence Rinder.</p>
<p>The Lutz Bacher piece is <i>Olympiad</i>, a beatifully damaged video of the 1936 Olympics Stadium in Berlin, made famous by another woman filmmaker.</p>
<p>The Tim Hawkinson piece, called <i>Seal</i>, looks like an official seal made from an elephant skin.</p>
