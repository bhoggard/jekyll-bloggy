<p>I love the pricing structure:</p>
<ul>
	<li>$90 nut only (no shell)</li>
	<li>$90 empty half shell</li>
	<li>$150 empty full shell</li>
	<li>$175 shell containing nut</li>
</ul>
<p>He has a show at <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/4267">Pierogi 2000</a> opening on Friday, titled &#8220;Iraq Rock,&#8221; described thusly</p>
<blockquote>
<p>This exhibition will feature an 8-by-8 foot tabletop model of a hypothetical benefit concert, Rock Iraq, which doesn&#8217;t exist but quite possibly could. It is a concert with corporate logos but no sponsors, no musicians, and no music. It exists as a tabletop model to American ambiguity, assuaging guilt, displaying compassion, condemning failure, and inventing triumph.</p>
</blockquote>
