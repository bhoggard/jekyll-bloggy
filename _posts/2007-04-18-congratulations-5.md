<p><a href="http://www.montagueprojects.com/">Julian Montague</a> is the winner of the <a href="http://www.thebookseller.com/control/?p=6&a=37373">Diagram Prize for the Oddest Title of 2006</a>, for his book <a href="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0810955202/tristanmedial-20"><em>The Stray Shopping Carts of Eastern North America</em></a>.  It is a book documenting his <a href="http://www.strayshoppingcart.com/">art project</a> of the same name.  Other books in the competition included <em>How Green Were the Nazis?</em> and <em>Spoon Boxes of Daghestan</em>.</p>
<p>There are a huge number of news articles on him winning the prize, including ones from <a href="http://books.guardian.co.uk/news/articles/0,,2056646,00.html"><em>The Guardian</em></a> and the <a href="http://www.cbc.ca/arts/books/story/2007/04/14/shoppingcart-book-prize.html?ref=rss"><span class="caps">CBC</span></a>.</p>
