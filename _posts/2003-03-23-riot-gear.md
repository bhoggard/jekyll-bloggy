<p>I got an email from Jesse of <a href="http://www.pith.org/">pith.org</a> fame &#8212; go read his beautifully written <a href="http://pith.org/pith/?20030323">post</a> from early this morning &#8212; about the fact that he only saw well-behaved police.  I agree that I only saw that, except that as I got close to the end I saw more and more police officers wearing riot gear on the site streets.  I finished the march around 3:30.</p>
<p>Tom Moody has a good photo of <a href="http://www.digitalmediatree.com/tommoody/?21226"><span class="caps">NYPD</span> in riot gear</a> from yesterday.  We all know displays like <a href="http://www.kottke.org/photos/nycprotest030321/index_44.html">this</a> are meant to intimidate, even when we&#8217;ve had hours of peaceful marching so far.</p>
