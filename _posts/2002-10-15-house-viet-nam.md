<p>I just read a <a href="http://www.nydailynews.com/news/col/jgonzalez/story/27103p-25712c.html">column</a> in the NY Daily News by Juan Gonzalez about Silvestre Reyes, a conservative Democrat from El Paso (and former Marine captain in Viet Nam, 1968-1969).</p>
<blockquote>&#8220;Thirty-five years ago, I found myself half a world away in a place called Vietnam,&#8221; Reyes said during the Iraq debate. Now, he went on, &#8220;mothers and fathers and veterans come to me and tell me, &#8216;Please, do not let us get back into a war without exhausting all other avenues.&#8217;&#8221;<br />
<br />
&#8220;As a member of the Committee on Intelligence, I have asked consistently the question. &#8230; What is the the connection between 9/11 and Iraq and Saddam Hussein? None.<br />
<br />
&#8220;What is the connection between Iraq and Saddam Hussein and Al Qaeda? Very little, if any. As to the weapons of mass destruction &#8230; there is a lot of speculation.&#8221;<br />
<br />
Even a top general who testified before the committee expressed severe doubts about a U.S. invasion of Iraq, Reyes said.<br />
<br />
Quite simply, the congressman concluded, &#8220;the case has not been made.&#8221;</blockquote>
<p>One of the other things that struck me, and should be bigger news, is this: 47 of the 52 members of the House that are either Hispanic or African-American voted against the Iraq war resolution.  The news media should be asking why.  Is it because they realize that their constituents will suffer so that more powerful people can get richer from the war?</p>
<blockquote>One reason for war you won&#8217;t hear from the White House is spelled O-I-L. Iraq has the world&#8217;s second-largest proven oil reserves after Saudi Arabia, about 11% of the world&#8217;s total.<br />
<br />
Kenneth Derr, the former <span class="caps">CEO</span> and chairman of Chevron, said it best in a 1998 speech at the Commonwealth Club of San Francisco.<br />
<br />
&#8220;Iraq possess huge reserves of oil and gas &#8211; reserves I&#8217;d love Chevron to have access to,&#8221; said Derr.</blockquote>
