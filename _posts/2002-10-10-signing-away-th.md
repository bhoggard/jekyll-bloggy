<p>Having relinquished the power to decide war and peace, the House <a href="http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/articles/A9998-2002Oct10.html">moves on</a>.  They have passed a resolution saying the President can decide when, or if, to attack another country.  I consider this abandonment of Congress&#8217;s right to declare war illegal.  Every person voting yes should be impeached.</p>
<p>As the Senate prepares to do the same, we have this ridiculous statement from Daschle:<br />
<blockquote>Across the Capitol, where the Senate was moving toward passage of the same resolution, Majority Leader Thomas A. Daschle (D-S.D.) said Americans will return attention to the economy &#8220;once we get this question of Iraq behind us.&#8221;</blockquote></p>
<p>How is going to war putting it &#8220;behind us&#8221;?</p>
