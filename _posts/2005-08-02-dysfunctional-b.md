<p>Here are two images from the dissolute budgies of a budgie &#8220;housing project&#8221; gone terribly wrong in this sculpture by Shaun Doyle &amp; Mally Mallinson in the current show at Cynthia Broan, titled <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/979">This Show is Ribbed For Her Pleasure</a>.  Luckily, <a href="/2002/11/parakeet-in-the.html">ours</a> is faring a bit better so far.</p>
<p>The exhibition is curated by Andrew Clarkin and Simon Pittuck, the Directors of <a href="http://www.re-title.com/exhibitions/keithtalentgallery.asp">Keith Talent Gallery</a> in East London.  Bonus points for anyone who gets the <a href="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0679730346/tristanmedial-20">reference</a> for the title of the gallery.</p>
<p>I also enjoyed the brightly colored ink jet prints by Oran O&#8217;Reilly and the sculptures by Adam Gillam, with the installation instructions written all over the works.</p>
