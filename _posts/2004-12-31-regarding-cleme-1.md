<p><a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/271">Regarding Clementine</a>, the next show at Clementine (one of the pioneers of Chelsea and still going strong), should be a lot of fun. It&#8217;s curated by <a href="http://www.choiresicha.com/">Choire Sicha</a>.</p>
