<p>On a happier note, we walked around the Englischer Garten and had lunch at the outside tables near the Chinese Pagoda.  There was a film crew of young, cute Germans eating near us, including this fine specimen, with a Brownsville(!) t-shirt:</p>
<p><img alt="brownsville.JPG" src="/mt/archives/brownsville.JPG" width="500" height="375" border="0" /></p>
<p>One of the things that amazes me about this part of the world is that they use glass everywhere.  They don&#8217;t give you plastic when you have a beer at an outside area on picnic tables &#8212; you get sturdy china plates and glass beer or wine glasses.  Even at an Esso (Exxon) gas station, you can drink coffee in a real cup and saucer.</p>
