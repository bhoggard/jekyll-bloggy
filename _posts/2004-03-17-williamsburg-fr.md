<p>We&#8217;re big fans of Carlos de Villasante&#8217;s work, as you can see from my <a href="https://bloggy.com/mt/search?search=carlos+de+villasante">various blog posts</a>.</p>
<p>He has a solo show at <a href="http://www.heriard-cimino.com/cx_frame.html">Heriard-Cimino Gallery</a> in New Orleans at the moment.  When we visited New Orleans a few years ago, the gallery was one of our first stops because the fabulous director, Jeanne Cimino, is a friend of <a href="http://www.margaretevangeline.com/">Margaret Evangeline</a>.</p>
<p>Closer to home, we&#8217;ll be headed out to Williamsburg on Friday for the <a href="http://www.williamsburggalleryassociation.com/">Williamsburg Gallery Association</a>&#8217;s Third Friday event.  All galleries will be open until 9pm.  It will give you a chance to see a few things I recommend.  Several of these are opening that night:</p>
<p>Suzanne Wright at <a href="http://www.monyarowegallery.com/">Monya Rowe</a> &#8211; extended through March 21</p>
<p>Reed Anderson at <a href="http://www.pierogi2000.com/flatfile/anderson04.html">Pierogi 2000</a></p>
<p>Christopher Johnson at <a href="http://www.plusultragallery.com/">Plus Ultra</a></p>
<p>Josh Stern at <a href="http://www.parkersbox.com/">Parker&#8217;s Box</a></p>
<p>The always fun <a href="http://www.brooklynfireproof.com/">Brooklyn Fire Proof</a> should be the place to end up, with their <a href="http://www.brooklynfireproof.com/events/aboutEvent.asp?ID=26">Fashion Social</a>.</p>
<p>Of course, this whole schedule may change drastically when we find out more details on <a href="http://www.beigerecords.com/cory/">Cory Arcangel</a>&#8217;s performance at the Whitney on Friday night.</p>
