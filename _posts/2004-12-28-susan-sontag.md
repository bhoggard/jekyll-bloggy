<p>Susan Sontag <a href="http://www.guardian.co.uk/arts/news/story/0,11711,1380505,00.html?gusrc=rss">has died</a>, aged 71.  She was born in here in New York, and died here.</p>
<p>She was one of the only &#8220;public intellectuals&#8221; I regularly encountered in New York, as she was someone, as James and I are, voraciously interested in art of all kinds, especially when it was new, or rare.  I would see her at a <span class="caps">BAM</span> performance of Frankfurt Ballet, or at an obscure play in the East Village, or at an <a href="http://www.findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_m1248/is_n5_v82/ai_15406242">art exhibit</a> related to the siege of Sarajevo at the New York Kunsthalle on East 5th Street.</p>
<p>We have one signed book of hers, but I will let James tell that story.</p>
