<p><em>1/16th of 100%!?</em>, video (23 minutes) <br />
Ina Diane Archer<br />
Writer, director, editor 1993/96</p>
<p>Montage that examines themes of appropriation, miscegenation and minstrelsy through manipulated footage found in Hollywood movies from the 1920s through the 1950s &#8212; including Imitation of Life, Showboat and The Jazz Singer.</p>
