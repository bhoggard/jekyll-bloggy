<p>On Friday, James and I attended the opening of the Sarah Peters show at <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/4881">Winkleman Gallery</a>.  We loved it so much that we did something we&#8217;ve rarely done lately &#8212; bought a new piece for the collection.  We also made it a &#8220;top pick&#8221; on <a href="http://www.artcal.net/">ArtCal</a>.  I titled this post &#8220;Full disclosure&#8221; as I don&#8217;t want people thinking we secretly set things to top picks to influence the market.  We&#8217;re not in the business of selling our art, and so far have never de-accessioned a piece.</p>
