<p>&#8230; by Christopher Dazey on media coverage of anti-war rallies.  Here is a sample, but you should <a href="http://www.salon.com/news/letters/2002/10/30/protest/index.html">read</a> the whole thing, plus the rest of the letters.  As a group, they are one of the best discussions of the art of protest &#8212; including all of the fringe groups and seeming off-topic speakers that often drive us all crazy when we attend one.</p>
<blockquote>Michelle Goldberg&#8217;s disappointment with the protesters in Washington over their apparent lack of a coherent message is akin to a campaign manager saying that her candidate should &#8220;stay on message.&#8221; However, the protesters were not running for office, and the language of corrupt, corporate politics should not be applied to grassroots movements.</blockquote>
