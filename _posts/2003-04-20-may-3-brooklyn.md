<p>I have always meant to attend one of Brooklyn Museum&#8217;s <a href="http://www.brooklynmuseum.org/programs/first_saturday">First Saturday</a> events, and this sounds like the one to choose.  Not only has the &#8220;Egypt Reborn&#8221; exhibit opened, but the entertainment includes:</p>
<blockquote>From 9 to 11 p.m., dance to Afro-beat and Middle-Eastern pop tunes spun by DJ Neva. A student of world music for 27 years, DJ Neva Wartell is resident DJ for &#8220;Nu World Soul,&#8221; a weekly world music dance party at Liquids in Manhattan. </blockquote>
<p>Newsday has an <a href="http://www.newsday.com/entertainment/printedition/ny-3244033apr20,0,10523.story?coll=ny%2Dentertainment%2Dprint">article</a> on the exhibit.</p>
