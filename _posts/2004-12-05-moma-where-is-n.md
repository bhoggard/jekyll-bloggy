<p>So, I never got around to writing my MoMA post, as <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/004512.html">James</a> did.  OK, here is one thought:</p>
<p>While I was intrigued to see how much of the work in the photography area had political/social/anti-war themes, I was surprised to see no work by Nan Goldin.  Does someone have an agenda?  I saw a whole wall for Cindy Sherman, and another for Philip-Lorca diCorcia.  While I appreciate both of them, I don&#8217;t think they are any more important than she is, or Mark Morrisroe is.</p>
<p>Slight non-sequitur: The only good thing about Trump Tower is that the tourists walking along Fifth Avenue in the afternoon seem lit and ready for a photo by Philip-Lorca diCorcia or <a href="http://www.beatstreuli.com/">Beat Streuli</a>.</p>
