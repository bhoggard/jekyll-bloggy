<p>I am so angry my hands are shaking.</p>
<p>I do not want one more American dollar going to Israel.  It is behaving like an apartheid state &#8212; persecuting the Palestinians with brutal force, and murdering people who try to prevent this outrage.</p>
<p>Yesterday an <span class="caps">IDF</span> (Israel Defense Forces) bulldozer ran over and killed an unarmed American woman, Rachel Corrie aged 23.  <a href="http://www.palsolidarity.org/rachel.htm">This page</a> has more information.  Be warned: there are graphic photographs of what happened to her, but I think it is very important that such picture are shown to the world.</p>
<p>We give <a href="http://www.us-israel.org/jsource/US-Israel/U.S._Assistance_to_Israel1.html">nearly $3 billion</a> a year to Israel at this point, and it&#8217;s being used to kill people.  They are requesting <a href="http://www.timesonline.co.uk/article/0,,5461-586184,00.html">an additional $12 billion</a> for this year.</p>
<p>I do not want the blood of Palestinians or anyone else on my hands.</p>
