<p>Yes, there&#8217;s <a href="http://www.artcal.net/">ArtCal</a> for making a list, but given the street layout of Williamsburg, we always use a map to plot out the most efficient course when we visit the galleries there.  And we always use a copy of <a href="http://www.wagmag.org/"><span class="caps">WAGMAG</span></a>.</p>
<p>This indispensable resource is having a fund raiser tonight.  It works the same as Momenta Art&#8217;s benefit: buy a ticket, and when your number is called you get to choose a work from among those remaining.  Tickets are $150.  You can view the works today from 1-6PM if you need a preview to make your list.</p>
<p>Reception and Raffle of work Saturday Dec. 16th, 7-9<br />
The <a href="http://www.frontroom.org/">Front Room</a> is at 147 Roebling St. <br />
(the corner of Roebling &amp; Metropolitan, Brooklyn).</p>
