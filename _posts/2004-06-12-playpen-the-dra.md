&#8220;Playpen&#8221; is a process-based exhibition that encourages experimentation with the boundaries of space, drawing, and the role of the institution. Each of the artists represented has created a project specifically for the exhibition. Often depending on direct interaction with the audience for their ultimate meaning, the works in &#8220;Playpen&#8221; cross the restricted line between the sanctity of objects and the temporality of performative events.<br />

The artists featured in &#8220;Playpen&#8221; are: David Brody (Brooklyn, NY), Voebe de Gruyter (Brussels, Belgium), Charles Goldman (Brooklyn, NY), Alina Viola Grumiller (New York, NY), Valerie Hegarty (Brooklyn, NY), Geoff Lupo (Brooklyn, NY), Edward Monovich (Summit, NJ), neuroTransmitter (New York, NY), Red76 (Portland, OR and Chicago, IL), Gedi Sibony (Brooklyn, NY), Austin Thomas (Brooklyn, NY), and Alex Villar (New York City).</blockquote>
