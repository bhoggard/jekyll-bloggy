<p>Friday night the 16th, we will be at the <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/3966">opening</a> of 31 Grand, newly relocated to 29th Street.   The first exhibition, titled &#8220;No New Tale To Tell&#8221;, is described thusly:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>inspired by the Love and Rockets song and our deep love of the narrative, this group show features a selection of our familiar artists, past and present, as well as some new discoveries. Please join us in celebrating these artists and our new location.  Artists: Karen Heagle, Alessandra Exposito, Fanny Bostrom, Mike Cockrill, Jon Elliott, Rachel Frank, Helen Garber, Lauren Gibbes, Magalie Guerin, Jason Clay Lewis, Francesca Lo Russo, Christa Parravani, Tom Sanford, Adam Stennett, and Barnaby Whitfield</p>
</blockquote>
<p><strong>Update</strong></p>
<p>The opening has been postponed.  I don&#8217;t have more details, but it&#8217;s probably moving-in difficulties, etc.</p>
