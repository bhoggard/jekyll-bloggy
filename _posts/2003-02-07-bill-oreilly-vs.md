<p><a href="http://www.livejournal.com/talkread.bml?journal=carpeicthus&itemid=238893">Carpeicthus  supplies us</a> with a transcript from Bill O&#8217;Reilly, where he attacks the son of a <span class="caps">WTC</span> victim for being unpatriotic, for bringing up the fact that we trained the mujahadeens in Afghanistan, who trained al Quaeda.</p>
<blockquote>&#8230;<br />
<br />
O&#8217;REILLY: You are mouthing a far left position that is a marginal position in this society, which you&#8217;re entitled to.<br />
<span class="caps">GLICK</span>: It&#8217;s marginal &#8212; right.<br />

<span class="caps">GLICK</span>: Well, actually, my father thought that Bush&#8217;s presidency was illegitimate.<br />
O&#8217;REILLY: Maybe he did, but&#8230;<br />
<span class="caps">GLICK</span>: I also didn&#8217;t think that Bush&#8230;<br />
O&#8217;REILLY: &#8230; I don&#8217;t think he&#8217;d be equating this country as a terrorist nation as you are.<br />
<span class="caps">GLICK</span>: Well, I wasn&#8217;t saying that it was necessarily like that.<br />
O&#8217;REILLY: Yes, you are. You signed&#8230;<br />
<span class="caps">GLICK</span>: What I&#8217;m saying is&#8230;<br />
O&#8217;REILLY: &#8230; this, and that absolutely said that.<br />
<span class="caps">GLICK</span>: &#8230; is that in &#8212; six months before the Soviet invasion in Afghanistan, starting in the Carter administration and continuing and escalating while Bush&#8217;s father was head of the <span class="caps">CIA</span>, we recruited a hundred thousand radical mujahadeens to combat a democratic government in Afghanistan, the Turaki government.<br />
O&#8217;REILLY: All right. I don&#8217;t want to&#8230;<br />
<span class="caps">GLICK</span>: Maybe&#8230;<br />
O&#8217;REILLY: I don&#8217;t want to debate world politics with you.<br />
<span class="caps">GLICK</span>: Well, why not? This is about world politics.<br />
O&#8217;REILLY: Because, No. 1, I don&#8217;t really care what you think.<br />
<span class="caps">GLICK</span>: Well, OK.<br />
