<p>I&#8217;m sorry I won&#8217;t get to see <a href="http://www.championfineart.org/exhibition/201">this show</a> in Los Angeles.  It&#8217;s at the wonderful Champion Fine Arts Gallery, which I discussed <a href="/2004/07/moving-party-be.html">here</a>.</p>
<p>Any show that is curated by artist/performer <a href="http://www.pinkghettoproductions.com/">Alix Lambert</a>, whom I finally met when she was performing in <a href="/2004/10/last-week-for-n.html">Nobody&#8217;s Lunch</a> by The Civilians, and includes work by our friend <a href="http://www.charlesgoldmanwork.com/">Charles Goldman</a>, is not to be missed.  It opens Saturday, February 26, from 6-8PM.</p>
