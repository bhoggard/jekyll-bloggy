<p><a href="http://www.andyschest.com">Andy</a> organized a get-together last night in honor of <a href="http://www.keithers.com/now/index.html">Keith</a> visit from L.A.  Also representing the left coast was the adorable <a title="don't miss the childhood photos" href="http://www.contrasts.net">Jessie</a>.  There were lots of attractive and smart boys there &#8212; no girls at all even though some were invited.</p>
<p>I finally got a chance to talk with <a href="http://www.ultrasparky.org">Dan</a>.  He&#8217;s a charming and smart guy &#8212; we talked about everything from ex-pat job opportunities in Europe to Benjamin Britten.  Note to Dan: we should get together and listen to some CDs.  I also listened to a discussion of font-geekitude between him and <a href="http://www.littleminx.com/x.html">Chad</a>.</p>
<p>The always squeezable <a href="http://www.revolutionfreedom.com/now.html">Dan&#8217;l</a> introduced me to a cool guy named John who runs the Streetwork project of <a href="http://www.safehorizon.org/">Safe Horizon</a>.  The group was the beneficiary of Dan&#8217;l&#8217;s recent blogathon.  John just happened to be there to meet a friend, and isn&#8217;t one of the blog-children, at least not yet.  We talked for quite a while.  He&#8217;s sexy and reminds me of a classical musician I once met in South Beach at <a href="http://www.southbeach-usa.com/columns/columns1/warsaw/warsaw.htm">Warsaw</a>.  Top that boys!  No circuit queens for me.</p>
<p>I also ran into Trick/Patrick of <a href="http://www.morplaymusic.com/">Morplay</a>.  I met him once before through <a href="http://www.artretran.com/newsite/EXHIBITIONS/Jun2000Sec/Bookstore/bookstore.html">Joe Ovelman</a>, so we talked about Joe&#8217;s art for a while.  I&#8217;m a huge fan, and I think James and I are among Joe&#8217;s most enthusiastic collectors.  There is a <a href="http://www.nerve.com/photography/ovelman/twoFaced/index.asp">Nerve gallery</a> of Joe&#8217;s work that&#8217;s worth the hassle of registering.</p>
<p>Who else was there?  I said hello to <a href="http://isobars.blogspot.com/" title="wish him happy birthday">Sam</a> and <a href="http://neuroticjew.blogspot.com/">Scott</a>, but we didn&#8217;t really talk.  I met <a href="http://g73.net/tite/" title="tall, cute">Brian</a>, who was wearing a t-shirt reminiscent of the playing-with-logo works of <a href="http://www.danielpflumm.com/">Daniel Pflumm</a>.</p>
<p>James has a few <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/002838.html">photos</a> on his site of the evening.</p>
