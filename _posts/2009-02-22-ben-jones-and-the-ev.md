<p>This is an installation shot from Ben Jones&#8217;s current <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/7/8827">show</a> at Deitch Projects, titled &#8220;The New Dark Age.&#8221;  As someone who has followed Ben&#8217;s work (via Paper Rad and solo projects) for years, I had to note that Kay Nine has gone from being fabricated from painted wood to now being made of marble and brass!  A good reflection of our current times.  Here is a photo of mine (and related <a href="/2005/03/good-stuff-last-1.html">post</a>) from his <a href="http://www.foxyproduction.com/exhibition/view/309">2005 show</a> at Foxy Production:</p>
