<p>Liza Sabater of <a href="http://www.culturekitchen.com/">CultureKitchen</a> and <a href="http://www.dailygotham.com/">Daily Gotham</a>, was checking out her stats when she noticed a link from the New York Times. They left the user registration system, open, so she registered and gave them a post to let them know they should secure their new blog.</p>
