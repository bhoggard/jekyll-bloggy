<p>There&#8217;s a nice <a href="http://www.salon.com/ent/music/feature/2002/05/30/waits/">essay</a> in Salon on the new Tom Waits CDs I just bought &#8211; <a HREF="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B00005YX3L/qid=1022788247/sr=8-1/tristanmedial-20">Alice</a> and  <a HREF="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B00005YX3K/qid=1022788247/sr=8-2/tristanmedial-20">Blood Money</a>.</p>
<p>Alice is from a musical theatre work created by Robert Wilson and my friend, the brilliant Paul Schmidt &#8212; his <a HREF="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0060928751/qid=1022788467/sr=1-3/tristanmedial-20">Chekhov translations</a> are highly recommended.</p>
<p>Blood Money is based on <a HREF="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0413140903/qid=1022788651/sr=1-1/tristanmedial-20">Buechner</a>&#8217;s Woyzeck.</p>
