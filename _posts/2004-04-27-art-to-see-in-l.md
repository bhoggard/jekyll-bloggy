<p>We just got a mailing about two shows at <a href="http://www.marcselwynfineart.com/">Marc Selwyn Fine Art</a> in Los Angeles curated by <a href="http://www.downtownarts.org/about.html">Simon Watson</a>/Scenic.</p>
<p>The first is a show of works by <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/003376.html">Paul P.</a>  We&#8217;re big fans of Paul&#8217;s work and own a painting plus a couple of works on paper.  The second is a group show that includes the likes of Christian Holstad, Terence Koh, Ann Craven, Wangechi Mutu, Scott Treleaven, and Joe Ovelman.</p>
<p>Both open May 1 (6-8pm) and run through June 19.</p>
