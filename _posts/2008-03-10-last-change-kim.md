<p>Today is the last day for the Kim Jones <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/6469">exhibition</a> at Pierogi.  I recommend a visit if you can fit it in today, as the painted photographs in the back room are some of my favorite works I&#8217;ve ever seen by him.  I think there is a bit of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Blake">William Blake</a> in these magical works.</p>
