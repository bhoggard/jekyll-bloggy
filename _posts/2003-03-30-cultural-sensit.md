<blockquote>Like it or not, Canadian baseball fans will be hearing God Bless America played during the seventh-inning stretch of each team&#8217;s first home game.<br />
<br />
Toronto&#8217;s SkyDome will air the song during the New York Yankees game on Monday night, a Toronto Blue Jays spokesman said. Normally, Okay, Blue Jays is played during the seventh-inning stretch.<br />
<br />
The directive was handed down from Major League Baseball, which has decreed that God Bless America will be sung during the seventh-inning stretch of all home openers, Sunday games and holiday games to honour U.S. military men and women serving in Iraq.</blockquote>
