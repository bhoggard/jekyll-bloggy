<p>Here&#8217;s a post for my geek readers.  <a href="http://www.artcal.net/">ArtCal</a> now uses <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/browse.html?node=16427261">Amazon S3</a> for all of the exhibition images.  That means we don&#8217;t have to worry about storage space as we continue to add shows, and that images should load more quickly.</p>
<p>Quite of the few of the ads are served that way too.  We just <span class="caps">DNS</span> magic to make it appear that they&#8217;re coming from hosts with names like adimages.tristanmedia.com and static.artcat.com.</p>
