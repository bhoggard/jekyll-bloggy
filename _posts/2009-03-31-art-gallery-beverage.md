<p>Lately I&#8217;ve seen a few gallery opening announcements where the beverage sponsor was Jamaican beer Red Stripe.  This makes a good &#8220;teaching moment.&#8221;  Jamaica is the most dangerous place in the Caribbean for queer people, with a government that regularly issues anti-<span class="caps">LBGT</span> statements, and a dance hall culture whose musicians regularly call for violence against queer people in their lyrics.  Much of Jamaica&#8217;s income comes from investment, trade, and tourism from the United States.  This is no time to do business with Red Stripe or for that matter Myers Rum.  Visit <a href="http://www.pamshouseblend.com/showDiary.do?diaryId=10125">Pam&#8217;s House Blend</a> and <a href="http://www.boycottjamaica.org/">Boycott Jamaica</a> for more information.</p>
