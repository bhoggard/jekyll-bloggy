One of the contributors is the harpist Helen Radice.  I nearly spit my coffee out onto my computer when I read <a href="http://harpist.typepad.com/twangtwangtwang/2004/08/wagner_book.html">this</a> on her personal weblog:</p>
<blockquote>&#8230; I would like to read a good book about Wagner and the Third Reich.<br />
<br />
Could anybody recommend one? There are so many I am rather at a loss, although the one entitled <i>Penetrating Wagner&#8217;s Ring</i> made me laugh so hard I had to leave Borders. </blockquote>
