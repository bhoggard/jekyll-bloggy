<p>The artist Benjamin Evans is the new Gallery Director of <a href="http://www.nurtureart.org/">NURTUREart</a>.  We first met him through <a href="http://www.momentaart.org/">Momenta</a>, where he was helping out with grant writing and the annual benefit.</p>
