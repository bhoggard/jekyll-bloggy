<p>As part of the Nancy Hwang birthday <a href="http://www.people.cornell.edu/pages/awa3">extravaganza</a>, I saw <a href="http://www.villagevoice.com/issues/0220/atkinson.php">Episode II: Attack of the Clones</a> last night.  Ugh &#8212; it was worse than I expected.  <a href="http://www.usaweekend.com/02_issues/020512/020512portman.html">Natalie Portman</a>&#8217;s a trooper for trying to act like this was a &#8220;real movie with acting&#8221; while saying some of the worst lines in movie history.  It made the 3 good ones (now known as Episodes 4-6) look like Shakespeare.  My friend Anees was right &#8211; it was like Star Wars Porn &#8212; all the stuff you like to see, over and over and over.  All money shots, all the time!</p>
<p>Whenever there were &#8220;plot&#8221; interludes, such as the scenes of the budding romance between Natalie and whoever that guy is, the audience snickered until we got back to the light sabers and special effects.</p>
<p>I miss <a href="http://www.fridae.com/magazine/ee20020515_1_1.php">Princess Leia</a>.</p>
