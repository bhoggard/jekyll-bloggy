<p>Super Merengue (SM)<br />
by Nicolás Dumit Estévez<br />
Thursday, November 13, 2003, 6:00-8:00 p.m. <br />
Location: The Newark Museum<br />
49 Washington Street, Newark<br />
<span class="caps">FREE</span> with Suggested Museum Admission Reservations are required;<br />
call 973-596-6613.</p>
<p>Board Merengue Air for a flight that leaves passengers suspended between the Dominican Republic and the United States. During the performance, participants learn about Caribbean migration patterns through music, ephemeral costumes, and altered emergency evacuation instructions.</p>
