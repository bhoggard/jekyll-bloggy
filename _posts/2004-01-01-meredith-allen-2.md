<p>I&#8217;ve been doing more work on <a href="http://www.meredithallen.com">Meredith</a>&#8217;s web page as the &#8220;guinea pig&#8221; for my artist web hosting business.  Now that she can add her own stuff, there are a lot more images.</p>

<p>That image is from her Williamsburg/Greenpoint snapshots.  It&#8217;s Lisa Schroeder of <a href="http://www.schroederromero.com/">Schroeder Romero</a> gallery, plus one of their artists, <a href="http://www.schroederromero.com/past/boyd.html">Robert Boyd</a>.</p>
<p>I really love her latest <a href="http://www.meredithallen.com/gallery/20">series</a>.  She has been taking photos of her mother&#8217;s Beanie Babies collection.  They are all wrapped in plastic bags, to preserve their market values.  I think they are quite beautiful, and the background information about her mother and the perversely commercial aspect of the items only makes them more interesting.</p>
