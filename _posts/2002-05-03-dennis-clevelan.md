<p>We saw <a href="http://www.lincolncenter.org/aboutLC/media_release.asp?version=&ws=&bc=3&PressReleaseID=202">Dennis Cleveland</a> last night.  This is the excellent opera &#8212; but not a Puccini-type opera &#8212; by Mikel Rouse that many of my friends have heard me rave about after it was done at <a href="http://www.thekitchen.org/">The Kitchen</a> a few years ago.  Here&#8217;s <a href="http://www.lincolncenter.org/search/event_details.asp?version=&ws=&bc=3&EventDateTimeID=2781&ProgramID=1&Srch=1&CompanyID=">more info</a> including a link to a video clip.  I don&#8217;t think the sound is that great on the video clip.</p>
<p>I think I liked it more at The Kitchen &#8212; cooler crowd than at something sponsored by Lincoln center, but I still highly recommend going.  You&#8217;re not going to see a work like this very often.</p>
