<p>The <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/6796">show</a> closed yesterday, but I wanted to put up an image and provide a <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/bloggy/tags/artcal6796/">link</a> to a few more photos I took.  The nest above was made through a rather laborious process of turning found paper and collage detritus into castable paper pulp.  That pulp was then used in molds made from real twigs.  My crooked photo of some of the process documentation is below.</p>
