<p>We attended the preview tonight of the <a href="http://www.nyartbookfair.com/">NY Art Book Fair</a> and had a great time.  I nearly ran out of money as I ran around buying books.  It runs through Sunday and is free for the rest of the weekend.  Don&#8217;t miss the upstairs, which is the more &#8220;alternative&#8221; part with <em>Friendly Fire</em>, described as &#8220;a curated selection of independent publishing by artists.&#8221;  It includes <span class="caps">LTTR</span>, Retard Riot, Cinders, Kate Glicksberg / Ridykeulous, and much more.  It was near these that we saw <a href="http://www.j-morrison.com/&#39;s">j. morrison</a> area with manpurses screened while you waited &#8212; viewer participation encouraged.</p>

<p>Afterwards we trudged home through the heavy rain, ate some pizza, and watched MTAA&#8217;s <a href="http://www.mteww.com/dc911/">DC 9/11 &#8211; The Evildoers&#8217; Remix</a>.  We got it via a donation to <a href="http://www.turbulence.org/fundraiser_06/index.html">Turbulence</a>.</p>
