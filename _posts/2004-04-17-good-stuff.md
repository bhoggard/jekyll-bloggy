<p>We saw two shows worth recommending in Chelsea today, before heading to the Paper Rad <a href="/2004/04/paper-rad-foxy.html">opening</a> &#8212; which is definitely recommended!</p>
<p>The first is <a href="http://www.axelraben.com/paperChase/pr/paperChase2.htm">Paper Chase</a> at Axel Raben, curated by Renee Riccardo.   Jon Rosenbaum&#8217;s little paper sculptures are magnificent, as are many other works in the show.</p>
<p>The second is <a href="http://www.josephmaida.com/">Joseph Maida</a> at <a href="http://www.wallspacegallery.com/">Wallspace</a>.  We realized after looking at his web site that we had seen one of his <a href="http://www.josephmaida.com/park.html">park</a> images in a group show at the gallery.  Also, he did the <a href="/2003/10/christian-holst.html">photo</a> of Christian Holstad that appeared in the New York Times Magazine last October.</p>
