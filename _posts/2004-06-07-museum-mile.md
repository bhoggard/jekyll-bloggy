<p>Tomorrow is <a href="http://www.museummilefestival.org/">Museum Mile Day</a> &#8211; 9 museums along Fifth Avenue open for free, 6-9pm, and the avenue closed to traffic.  It&#8217;s one of my favorite art things in <span class="caps">NYC</span>.  Great crowd, great vibe.</p>
<p>The <a href="http://www.metmuseum.org/special/index.asp?HomePageLink=special_c4">Metropolitan</a> has August Sander, the Byzantium show, the show of 18th century French fashion and furniture, and more.  The <a href="http://www.goethe.de/uk/ney/enmuseum.htm">Goethe Institute</a> always has some interesting stuff too.  Oddly, the <a href="http://www.neuegalerie.org/neuemain.html">Neue Galerie</a> web site says that their expressionism show closes today.</p>
