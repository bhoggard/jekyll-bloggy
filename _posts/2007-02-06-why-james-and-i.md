<p>A lot of friends seemed surprised that we weren&#8217;t more excited after the last election, when the Democrats took back both houses of Congress.  That&#8217;s because we were expecting Congress to behave as it is now.</p>
<p><a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/006045.html">Senate votes not to debate Iraq proposal</a></p>
<p>My first comment upon reading this was: &#8220;It&#8217;s bad for troop morale to talk about not adding more troops, but getting them all killed for no effective purpose is supporting them?&#8221;</p>
<p>Meanwhile, yes we have raised the minimum wage, but the Democrats have done nothing on the suspension of habeas corpus, torture, <span class="caps">CIA</span> black sites, illegal eavesdropping, and Guantanamo Bay.  Color me not impressed.</p>
<p>Related:  Another reason why I rarely link to Daily Kos.  His <a href="http://www.dailykos.com/story/2007/2/5/191529/7076">reaction</a> is that this event in the Senate is helpful for the 2008 elections.  I think things might get a bit worse before then.  This isn&#8217;t just about electoral tactics.</p>
