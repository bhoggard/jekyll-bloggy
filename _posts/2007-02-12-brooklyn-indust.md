<p>I just received a press release from them (at <a href="http://www.artcal.net/">ArtCal</a>) announcing that their store at Broadway and Driggs in South Williamsburg has been converted to an art space, with the <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/4098">first exhibition</a> opening on February 23rd.  The show includes video artist <a href="http://www.pierogi2000.com/flatfile/gilmorek.html">Kate Gilmore</a>.</p>
<blockquote>
<p>Brooklyn Industries, an artist-owned design and apparel company, will open its first permanent contemporary art exhibition space, Brooklyn Industries Contemporary Art, with the exhibition <em>Out of the Loop</em>. The exhibition will feature video work by Vahap Avsar, Selim Birsel, Lexy Funk, Kate Gilmore, Noritoshi Hirakawa, and Nasan Tur.</p>
</blockquote>
