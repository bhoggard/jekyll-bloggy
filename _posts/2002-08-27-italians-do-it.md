<p>Sometimes it&#8217;s a mess, and it&#8217;s not the place to be when you have to get something done immediately, but I love Italy anyway.  The latest reason &#8212; found on <a href="http://www.zoomata.com/modules.php?name=News&file=article&sid=688">zoomata</a>:</p>
<blockquote> Italian men on the beach trying to pick up women are relying on the heavyweights of literature instead of bodybuilding.<br />
<br />
According to a magazine survey of over 1,000 Italian men, some 68% of those aged 18- 55 take a book to the beach in hopes of catching the eye of a bathing beauty.<br />
<br />
Most have a very clear idea about what sort of literature seduces &#8212; eighty percent of these tome-carrying Romeos rely on the Divine Comedy and the Bible.<br />
<br />
Other pickup favorites were Giacomo Leopardi&#8217;s poems and Alessandro Manzoni&#8217;s epic love story &#8220;The Betrothed.&#8221; Although some 27% admitted favoring books instead of the usual crossword puzzle or gossip magazine because it&#8217;s important to appear &#8216;cultured,&#8217; around 20% said the books were also a source of inspiration &#8212; for pickup lines.</blockquote>
