<p>Fred &#8220;God hates Fags&#8221; Phelps, the man famous for picketing funeral of people who have died of <span class="caps">AIDS</span> and the Matthew Shepard funeral, arrived with his little group to protest the first day of school at <a href="/2003/09/monday-harvey-m.html">Harvey Milk High School</a>.</p>
<p>There are a lot more anti-Phelps protesters &#8212; we arrived at 7:30am and there was already a huge crowd.</p>
<p>I have a <a href="https://bloggy.com/gallery/phelps-harvey-milk">gallery</a> with a few photos I took.  Highlights of the morning:</p>
<ul><li>The crowd cheered the school kids as they walked past us to enter the school.</li>
<li>The Police actually moved the barricades to give us more room, and put us closer to the Phelps gang across the street, when the &#8220;<span class="caps">FAGS</span> <span class="caps">FDNY</span>&#8221; and &#8220;Thank God for 9/11&#8221; signs appeared.  I&#8217;m sure they also appreciated it when the Phelps people brought out the American flags to stand on.</li>
<li>Someone yelled out, &#8220;Love the &#8221;http://bloggy.com/gallery/phelps-harvey-milk/phelps&quot;&gt;purple jacket</a>, Fred!&quot;</li>
<li>Around 8:15 black towncars arrived and took most of the Phelps people away.  They used a car service!</li>
<li>There was one heavy guy on the Phelps side wearing a <a href="http://www.snopes.com/legal/twinkie.htm">Twinkie box</a> as a hat.</li>
<li>They used printed lyrics to help them out while singing.</li>
<li>There was a big garbage truck next to them, being loaded with demolition debris, for most of the demo.  When it left around 8:30, people yelled, &#8220;Hey! Your ride is leaving!&#8221;</li>
</ul>
<p>I&#8217;ll be back there at 3:00 when the kids get out of school.</p>
