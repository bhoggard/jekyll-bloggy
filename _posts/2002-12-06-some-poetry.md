<p>I get regular emails of poetry from <a href="http://www.scn.org/arts/realpoetik/">RealPoetik</a>.  They don&#8217;t put the stuff they send out on the web right away, so I&#8217;ll post the latest one, from Stacie Barrie:</p>
<p>I Sold You, You Sold Me<br />
(a poem in Newspeak)</p>
<p>From the age of pavement<br />
From the age of split seconds I salute:<br />
Not files<br />
Not papers<br />
Not pictures<br />
No need<br />
No mini<br />
No maxi<br />
No need<br />
Minus time<br />
Minus space<br />
Plus persons<br />
Plus statues<br />
Plus corporations undead<br />
Freeway, landfill, proving grounds<br />
Newthink bellyfeels prolefeed<br />
Old behind<br />
Old below<br />
Oldthink always new<br />
Victory is apathy</p>
