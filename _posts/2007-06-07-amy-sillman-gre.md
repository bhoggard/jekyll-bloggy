<p>This looks like a good thing to attend tonight.  We&#8217;re stopping by before hitting a play at <a href="http://www.atlantictheater.org/">Atlantic Theater</a>.  A.R.T. was the creation of the late, great <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/005105.html">Bill Bartman</a>.</p>
<blockquote>Please join us Thursday, June 7th from 5:00 &#150; 7:00 pm at Printed Matter to celebrate the publication of the fourth volume of Between Artists: Amy Sillman &amp; Gregg Bordowitz. <br />
<br />
Between Artists is a series of small format, conversation based books edited by Alejandro Cesarco. The books document different positions and strategies of leading and up-and-coming contemporary artists. <br />
<br />
Printed Matter is located at 195 Tenth Avenue between 21st and 22nd streets. The artists will be present.<br />
