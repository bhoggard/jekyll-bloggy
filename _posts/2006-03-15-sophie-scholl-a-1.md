<p>We saw <em>Sophie Scholl</em> at <a href="http://www.filmforum.org/films/sophie.html">Film Forum</a> yesterday.  It&#8217;s a very powerful film about using one&#8217;s conscience when everyone else is trying to just &#8220;get by&#8221; in a totalitarian state.  I highly recommend seeing it.  It runs through the end of the month.</p>
