<p>I ignored Dan as he kept screaming about Ralph Nader supposedly costing Al &#8220;can&#8217;t wait to talk about God and morality again&#8221; Gore the election.</p>
<p>Now he&#8217;s ranting about how liberals should be <span class="caps">FOR</span> the war in Iraq because it will liberate the Iraqi population from a terrible dictator.  He thinks this administration is capable of pulling of a post-<span class="caps">WWII</span> Japan trick on Iraq!</p>
<p>Did aliens replace his brain?  It&#8217;s more fun to read David Ehrenstein&#8217;s <a href="http://fablog.ehrensteinland.com/archives/000018.shtml#000018">discussion</a> of it than it is to read the actual column.</p>
<p>Oh, and he&#8217;s <a href="http://www.thestranger.com/2002-09-19/feature2.html">tired of worrying about <span class="caps">AIDS</span> too</a>:</p>
<blockquote>&#8220;I used to think about <span class="caps">AIDS</span> all the time, read about <span class="caps">AIDS</span> constantly, and do a lot of writing about <span class="caps">AIDS</span>. But in the last few years, I&#8217;ve found it harder and harder to give a shit about <span class="caps">AIDS</span>. Instead, I want to write about the monorail or Iraq &#8212; this despite the fact that two of my best friends in the whole world are infected with <span class="caps">HIV</span>.&#8221;</blockquote>
