<p>I don&#8217;t know if I will get into trouble for saying this, but the article mentions works by John Currin and Lisa Yuskavage going for as much as $1 million each.  I find both artists&#8217; work kitsch at best, and basically dreck.  I can&#8217;t believe the crap people will buy once it has some kind of imprimator.</p>
