<p>The show is closed, but I wanted to put up a couple of photos I took on the last day of the installation <a href="http://www.artcat.com/exhibits/10553">Drippy</a> by <a href="http://www.godwardsculpture.com/">Ben Godward</a> and <a href="http://famousaccountants.wordpress.com/">Famous Accountants</a> (Ellen Letcher and Kevin Regan), all of whom I met via the wonder that was/is <a href="http://www.pocketutopia.com/">Pocket Utopia</a>.  The &#8220;dripping blood&#8221; on the walls is by Famous Accountants, and the site-specific sculpture in the middle is by Ben Godward.</p>
