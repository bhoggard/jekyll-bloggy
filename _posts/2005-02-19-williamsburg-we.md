<p>The wonderful collective <a href="http://www.open-ground.org/">Open Ground</a> will be having its <a href="http://www.open-ground.org/cgi-bin/wiki.rb/Williamsburg%20Wedding.html">final exhibition</a> in March, as the members move on to other projects, including &#8220;including developing an online arts magazine and innovative resources for art lovers.&#8221;</p>
<p>The last show is their half of an artists&#8217; exchange with the Berlin gallery <a href="http://www.scherer8.de/">Galerie Scherer8</a>.  There is an opening/party on March 12.</p>
<p>[image from the Open Ground web site &#8212; Christian Grosskopf is a German artist from Berlin who creates work in figurative and landscape painting and works on paper inspired by early American animation.]</p>
