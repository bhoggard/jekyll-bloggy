<p>A friend working at an unnamed company told me about the (widely unloved) <span class="caps">CEO</span> bringing in yet another IT consultant, probably on a search and destroy mission to get more control over their tech people.  He hired them to come in while the <span class="caps">CIO</span> was away on vacation.  Nice.</p>
<p>Here is what they had to say:</p>
<blockquote>The web site is fine, technically and architecturally.  Your IT guys are quite competent &#8212; save you a great deal of money &#8212; and are &#8216;probably more competent than you deserve.&#8217;</blockquote>
<p>The good guys (and gals) should win more often.</p>
