<p>We saw Monteverdi&#8217;s Orfeo last night at <a href="http://www.bam.org/"><span class="caps">BAM</span></a>.  <span class="caps">VERY</span> disappointing.  The music was OK, but the direction (by Diane Paulus of <a href="http://www.thedonkeyshow.com/">Donkey Show</a> fame) was terrible.  The sets weren&#8217;t very attractive, and except for a few exceptions the costumes were ugly.</p>
<p>Only one of the 3 operas in BAM&#8217;s Monteverdi cycle &#8211; Il Ritorno D&#8217;Ulisse &#8211; was great.  The other one (L&#8217;Incoronazione di Poppea) was also terrible.  Making opera boring (cf. <a href="http://www.metopera.org/">The Met</a>) is a crime!</p>
