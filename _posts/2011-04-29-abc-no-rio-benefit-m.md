<p><img alt="abc-no-rio.gif" src="/2011/04/29/images/abc-no-rio.gif" width="614" height="272" class="mt-image-none" /></p>

<p>Come join me and <a href="http://www.jameswagner.com/">James</a> at <a href="http://www.abcnorio.org/"><span class="caps">ABC</span> No Rio</a>&#8217;s <a href="http://www.abcnorio.org/benefit2011/">benefit</a> on May 3rd at <a href="http://www.allegralaviola.com/">Allegra LaViola</a> on the Lower East Side. There will be wine, beer, food, a great silent art auction, and guest DJs including <a href="http://www.annakustera.com/">Anna Kustera</a>, <a href="http://magazine.saatchionline.com/author/dougmclement">Doug McClemont</a>, and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kembra_Pfahler">Kembra Pfahler</a>.</p>
<p>Tickets start at $50.</p>
<p>James and I are on the benefit committee for this amazing collectively-run center for art and activism founded in 1980 &#8212; initially as a squat where they took over an abandoned building to put on an art show called <em><a href="http://www.abcnorio.org/about/history/res_statement_80.html">Real Estate</a></em>.</p>

<p><img alt="abc-no-rio-facade.jpg" src="/2011/04/29/images/abc-no-rio-facade.jpg" width="338" height="500" class="mt-image-none" /></p>

<p>The old tenement building is going away to be replaced by a <a href="http://www.abcnorio.org/newbuilding.php">new building</a> by architect Paul Castrucci based on sustainable design principles. Even if you can&#8217;t make it to the benefit, any donation would be most appreciated!</p>
