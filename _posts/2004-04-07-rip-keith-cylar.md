<p>Keith Cylar, co-founder of <a href="http://www.housingworks.org/">Housing Works</a>, has died.  I have never known of an organization that started out as a grass-roots activist organization and grew into something serving so many people while keeping its activist credentials.  They have always helped the people &#8212; drug users, people with <span class="caps">AIDS</span> &#8212; that the other service and homeless organizations didn&#8217;t want to deal with.</p>
