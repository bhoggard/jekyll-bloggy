<p>This artist&#8217;s work was a new discovery for us at the <span class="caps">LMCC</span> Redheard Gallery&#8217;s show, <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/8/4222"><em>Imagined Worlds</em></a>.  The artist creates these works, described as &#8220;compound photographs&#8221; by cutting out pieces from proof sheets to compose a new work.  They&#8217;re hard to photograph, but I hope this detail gives an idea of the piece.  Good stuff.</p>
