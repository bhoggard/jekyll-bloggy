<p>I heard her perform during the Howl! festival in a program organized by Chris Rael of <a href="http://www.churchofbetty.com/">Church of Betty</a>, also known as Mr. <a href="http://www.pennyarcade.com/bio.html">Penny Arcade</a>.  She is a warm performer, with rather orchestral writing for such a small ensemble.  I liked the way she told the crowd which musician was most featured in each song they performed.</p>
<p>Here is an MP3 of her cover of Earth, Wind and Fire&#8217;s <a href="https://bloggy.com/mp3/rebecca_moore-fantasy.mp3">Fantasy</a>, from the <a href="http://www.bluviolin.com/homewreckordings.html">Home Wreckordings 1997-1999</a> album.  Also check out a rambling, amusing write-up on <a href="http://www.epinions.com/content_106906750596">epinions.com</a>.</p>
