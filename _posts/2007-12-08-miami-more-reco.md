<p>I&#8217;m no <a href="http://www.artfagcity.com/">Paddy Johnson</a> when it comes to covering the art fairs in nearly real time in Miami, so here are some quick recommendations without links based on what we&#8217;ve seen so far:</p>
<p>Go see <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/006786.html">James</a> for info on ArtHaus.</p>
<p><strong>Aqua (hotel version)</strong></p>
<ul>

	<li>Platform &#8211; William Powhida&#8217;s paintings and video, Carl Ferrero&#8217;s foot fetish watercolors, and Melissa Pokorny&#8217;s sculptures</li>
	<li>Road Agent &#8211; Ludwig Schwart&#8217;z video &#8220;Untitled (Zombies)&#8221; and Ryan Humphrey&#8217;s painted <span class="caps">BMW</span> hood</li>
	<li>Morgan Lehman &#8211; David Allee&#8217;s asylum photos</li>

	<li>Howard House &#8211; Cat Clifford&#8217;s videos and drawings</li>
	<li><span class="caps">CTRL</span> &#8211; Laura Braciale&#8217;s paintings</li>

</ul>
