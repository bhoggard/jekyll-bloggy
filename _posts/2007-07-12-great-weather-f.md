<p>James and I, however, will be on Ludlow Street for the re-opening of <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/6/5053">31 Grand</a>.  <strike>How could we possibly miss a <a href="http://barnabywhitfield.com/">Barnaby Whitfield</a> painting that has <a href="http://corncub.blogspot.com/">Nicole Eisenman</a> and <a href="http://tom-sanford.blogspot.com/2007/06/31-grand-is-back.html">Tom Sanford</a> (source of image) in it?</strike> [Barnaby informs me this won&#8217;t be in the show, but it&#8217;s too great not to leave in the post.]</p>
