<p>While we were traveling, <a href="http://www.artcat.com/">ArtCat</a> got its first artist outside of the U.S., <a href="http://www.davidkefford.com/">David Kefford</a>.  The work I&#8217;ve seen so far looks quite interesting, so I&#8217;m excited about this!</p>
<p>His about page says:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>David Kefford is an artist who uses low-tech craft processes to transform &#8220;redundant and un-loved&#8221; found and ordinary objects into surreal creations imbued with human characteristics and emotions.</p>
</blockquote>
