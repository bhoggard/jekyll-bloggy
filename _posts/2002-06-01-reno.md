<p>We saw <a href="http://www.citizenreno.com/rebel.html">Reno</a> tonight at the <a href="http://www.atthezipper.com/">Zipper Theater</a> on West 37th.  Cool space, fabulous show.  It&#8217;s a bit more &#8220;produced&#8221; than when I saw her at <a href="http://www.lamama.org/">La Mama</a>.  I love her, and highly recommend seeing it.  She hates Telecharge as much as the rest of us, so you can go buy tickets at the box office instead.</p>
<p>Afterward we went to <a href="http://newyork.citysearch.com/profile/7139819/">Market Cafe</a>.  Bargain!  $12 entrees, great wine list, and a waitress that reminded me of <a href="http://www.nerve.com/Dispatches/bedroomInterview/sedaris/">Amy Sedaris</a>.  I ordered a bottle of Morgon (pronouncing it the French way), and she said &#8211; oh, you mean Morgo<u>n</u>, like Morgon David.</p>
