<p>A big percentage of <span class="caps">NYPD</span> officers live outside of the city.  They probably don&#8217;t know why it matters when they block a crosswalk for 15 minutes to talk to someone on the sidewalk.  There was even enough space to back up and not block it when I first walked by.</p>
<p>Does anyone know an up-to-date source on the percentage that live in the city?  I see a <a href="http://nypdconfidential.com/columns/1997/970407.html">1997 estimate</a> of 45%.</p>
