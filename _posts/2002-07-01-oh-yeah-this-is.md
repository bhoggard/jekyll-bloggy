<p><a href="http://sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?f=/n/a/2002/07/01/national0322EDT0434.DTL&nl=fix">In Alabama this weekend</a>:</p>
<p>An evangelist who was asked to sing at his wife&#8217;s uncle&#8217;s funeral claims he had a revelation from God that led him to insult mourners and say that the dead man was damned.</p>
<p>Orlando Bethel said he spoke words that &#8220;the Lord revealed to me.&#8221; Preaching over a microphone at the Greater Pine Grove Baptist Church, he told some 100 mourners they were &#8220;fornicators&#8221; and &#8220;whoremongers.&#8221; He said the deceased, Lish Devan Taylor, had gone to hell.</p>
