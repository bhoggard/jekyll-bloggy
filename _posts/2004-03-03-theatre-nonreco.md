<p>Ugh.  We saw Nicky Silver&#8217;s <i>Beautiful Child</i> at <a href="http://www.vineyardtheatre.org/">Vineyard Theatre</a> last night.</p>
<p>I hated it so much I don&#8217;t even want to talk about it.  If I hadn&#8217;t been on the second row I might have walked out.</p>
<p>I was actually angry when I left the theatre.  It&#8217;s bad enough to see a mess of a play that&#8217;s a comedy, but to see an awful play about a child molester is just too much.  If I had been The Vineyard, I would have told Nicky, &#8220;we&#8217;re not producing this one.&#8221;  It was like  bad dinner theatre Albee.</p>
<p>I wonder what Rex Reed thought?  He was in the audience.</p>
