<p>Good site.  <a href="http://costofwar.com/">costofwar.com</a> keeps track of the dollar cost of the Iraq war so far, and compares it to other things we could be spending it on.  An example: we could have provided health insurance for about 50 million children for one year with the amount spent so far.</p>
