<p>I was reading an <a href="http://www.newsday.com/news/nationworld/world/wire/sns-ap-germany-hitlers-retreat,0,2547196.story?coll=sns-ap-world-headlines">article</a> on a new luxury hotel to open March 1 in Berchtesgaden, site of Adolf Hitler&#8217;s Alpine retreat.  I was struck by this part:</p>
<blockquote>The decision to build a hotel on the site above the German Alp town of Berchtesgaden angered many Jewish groups.<br />
<br />
Officials have tried to address their concerns with a documentation center opened in 1999 to detail the area&#8217;s Nazi past. In addition, the state of Bavaria kept ownership of the land and set the condition that the hotel be designed for affluent tourists &#8212; precautions designed to help keep out neo-Nazis.</blockquote>
<p>Using wealth restrictions to keep out people with far-right beliefs?  Wouldn&#8217;t work here!</p>
