<p><a href="http://www.wired.com/news/politics/0,1283,56860,00.html">Keeping Track of John Poindexter</a></p>
<blockquote>The head of the government&#8217;s Total Information Awareness project, which aims to root out potential terrorists by aggregating credit-card, travel, medical, school and other records of everyone in the United States, has himself become a target of personal data profiling. <br />
<br />
Online pranksters, taking their lead from a San Francisco journalist, are publishing John Poindexter&#8217;s home phone number, photos of his house and other personal information to protest the <span class="caps">TIA</span> program. </blockquote>
