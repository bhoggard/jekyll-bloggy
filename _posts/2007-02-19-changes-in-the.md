<p>Sixtyseven Gallery has rechristened itself <a href="http://thierrygoldberg.com/">Thierry Goldberg Projects</a>.  They will now be located on the Lower East Side, at 5 Rivington Street near The Bowery, right by my favorite German restaurant, <a href="http://www.loreleynyc.com/">Loreley</a>.  The first exhibition is a solo show by Swetlana Heger, and opens March 1, 6-9PM.</p>
<p>The name of the gallery comes from the maiden names of the two principals&#8217; mothers.</p>
