<p>Jeffy Whitty (of Avenue Q fame) has a play, <i>The Hiding Place</i>, at <a href="http://www.atlantictheater.org/">Atlantic Theater</a> (the 16th Street location) through January 25.  He manages to be very funny plus put in some very smart commentary on theater, the visual arts, and the literary world.  The whole cast is great, but I really loved Susan Parfour, and I would go see Kate Blumberg in anything.</p>
