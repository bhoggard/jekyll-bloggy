<p>The Nando <a href="http://www.nandotimes.com/entertainment/story/391709p-3110447c.html">story</a> says&#8230;</p>
<blockquote>Widespread sharing of songs over the Internet does not reduce the overall amount spent on music purchases, and may even increase it, according to a study released Friday.<br />
<br />
The report by Jupiter Media Metrix, a high-tech consulting firm, challenges the assertions of the major music labels that the industry is being victimized by piracy of music over the Internet.</blockquote>
