<p>As I&#8217;ve heard <a href="http://gaytoday.badpuppy.com/garchive/people/102797pe.htm">Ann Northrop</a> say more than once, &#8220;How do they sleep at night?&#8221;.</p>
<p>Bob Barr, Georgia Republican and the House impeachment manager during the Clinton impeachment travesty, is <a href="http://www.salon.com/politics/feature/2002/06/14/barr/">suing</a> Clinton, James Carville and Larry Flint for $30 million, claiming &#8220;loss of reputation and emotional distress&#8221;.  (This is a Salon Premium article, so unless you&#8217;re a subscriber you&#8217;ll only see the beginning.)</p>
<p>Barr has quietly filed a suit against Clinton, Carville and Flynt for &#8220;participating in a common scheme and unlawful on-going conspiracy to attempt to intimidate, impede and/or retaliate against [Barr]&#8221; for his role as an impeachment manager in 1999.</p>
<p>At the same time, he&#8217;s championing a bill that would limit non-economic medical damages to $250,000, saying &#8220;a national liability insurance crisis is ravaging the nation&#8217;s healthcare system.&#8221;</p>
<p>There&#8217;s a mention of the lawsuit in the <a href="http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/articles/A43164-2002Jun13.html">Washington Post</a>, but not the bill limiting damages.</p>
<p>Speaking of Bob Barr&#8230; here is a nice little <a href="http://www.inthesetimes.com/issue/25/16/appallo2516.html">anecdote</a> that was reported on the local <span class="caps">NBC</span> affiliate, plus the Alanta Journal and Constitution, about him calling an airport guard an &#8220;idiot little nigger&#8221;.</p>
