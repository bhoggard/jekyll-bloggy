<blockquote>Chalk it up to the January blues, or having to go back to work after a two-week vacation, or simply a plate full of weighty, as-yet-unmade decisions.<br />
<br />
&#8230;<br />
<br />
Journalists escorted into a Cabinet meeting on Monday were allowed just four questions. On Wednesday, the media were ushered in at the beginning of a session with congressional leaders from both parties. Bush tersely informed them that no questions would be welcomed.<br />
<br />
&#8220;I&#8217;m going to have a statement and then we&#8217;ll ask you to leave so we can get down to our business,&#8221; he said. After his statement and the signing of legislation extending federal unemployment benefits, he reiterated the point. &#8220;Get out of the room as quickly as you came in,&#8221; he said. </blockquote>
