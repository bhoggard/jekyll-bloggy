<p>When I took yesterday&#8217;s <a href="/2007/09/skateboards-wes.html">photo</a>, many people on 23rd Street were waiting to be allowed to go east while the police had the area around 23rd and Seventh blocked off for a bomb scare.  It&#8217;s the second time this has happened in 23rd Street this month.  The last one was west of Eighth Avenue.  I love this comment on a <a href="http://legends.typepad.com/blogchelsea/2007/09/bomb-scare-bumm.html">blogchelsea post</a> about yesterday:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>We live in an age of paranoia. Suspicious package? We used to call that &#8220;garbage.&#8221;</p>
</blockquote>
