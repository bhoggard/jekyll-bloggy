<p>I stopped my subscription to <a href="http://www.economist.com/">The Economist</a> last year because I felt they were being intellectually dishonest with their unquestioning backing of Bush and the attack on Iraq.  I always knew they were a relatively conservative news magazine, but I thought of them as principled, and a good source of non-<span class="caps">USA</span> news.  I felt betrayed by their attitude towards the Bush administration.</p>
<p>Things seem to be looking up on that front.</p>
