<p>Some more quick notes, before I head out to the <span class="caps">DIVA</span> fair and Apex Art:</p>
<p>Two of the best overall gallery booths I visited were Alison Jacques from London &#8212; including Dean Sameshima, Jon Pylypchuk, and a wall of Mapplethorpes selected by David Hockney &#8212; and Dallas-based <a href="http://www.angstromgallery.com/">Angstrom Gallery</a> with a cool video/sound/movement sculpture by Jeff Shore and John Fisher, plus work by Kevin Landers, Ann Craven, Paul P. (including his first prints!), and outsourced-to-China editioned paintings by Ludwig Schwarz.</p>
<p>Oh, and someone needs to buy me the Mary Heilman paintings at Kenny Schachter.</p>
