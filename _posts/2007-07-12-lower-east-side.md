<p>I will admit I haven&#8217;t seen this work in person yet, but from what I&#8217;ve seen online, <a href="http://www.exitart.org/">Exit Art</a> and the <a href="http://www.efa1.org/">Elizabeth Foundation for the Arts</a> are not the only art spaces worth visiting in the West 30s these days.  Despite its name, the <a href="http://www.printshop.org/">Lower East Side Printshop</a> is now located on West 37th Street between Eighth and Ninth Avenues.  Here is an installation view and three details by Matthew Day Jackson from the <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/13/5003">current show</a> (up through August 31).</p>
