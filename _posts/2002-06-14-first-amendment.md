<p>Under Mayor Giuliani, municipal workers were forbidden to talk to the press without permission.  Not surprisingly, the city lost a lawsuit over this.  Now that we have a mayor who has heard of the Bill of Rights, the city <a href="http://www.newsday.com/news/local/wire/ny-bc-ny--cityspeechlimits0613jun13.story?coll=ny%2Dap%2Dregional%2Dwire">won&#8217;t appeal the decision</a>.</p>
