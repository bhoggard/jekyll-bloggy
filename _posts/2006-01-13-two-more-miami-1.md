<p>At Pulse we saw two things I forgot to mention.  First, an artist from <a href="http://www.connercontemporary.com/">Conner Contemporary</a> (Washington, DC) named Zach Storm &#8212; no, really.  The work we saw consisted of clever drawings of posters for fictional blockbuster movies.  If you&#8217;re a fan of <a href="/2005/12/dam-stuhltrager.html">William Powhida</a>, I think you&#8217;ll like Storm&#8217;s work.</p>
