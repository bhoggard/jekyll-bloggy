<p>There is an event at <a href="http://www.artcal.net/location/view/1/274">Eyebeam</a> tonight as part of their <em>Produced at Eyebeam: Work in Process</em> exhibition:</p>
<blockquote><strong>January 26, 6:30-8:30pm &#8211; An Evening with the Artists</strong><br />
Please join us for an evening with the Artists in Residence exhibiting in Work in Process. Following an overview by Benjamin Weil, Eyebeam&#8217;s Curatorial Chair, the public is invited for an informal tour and reception. The artists will be on hand to talk one on one about their process and their ongoing projects.</blockquote>
