<p>From <a href="http://newyorkbusiness.com/news.cms?newsId=9459">Crain&#8217;s</a></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Mayor Michael Bloomberg on Thursday announced the establishment of the Edward I. Koch scholarships at the City College of New York, the former mayor&#8217;s alma mater.</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>The announcement came during festivities at Gracie Mansion marking Mr. Koch&#8217;s 80th birthday.</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>According to college officials, a $100,000 gift will be used to establish five 4-year scholarships at City College. The college has yet to determine who will be eligible for the scholarships, although it is likely that they will be for undergraduates. The identity of the donor was not disclosed.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>No word yet on whether the criteria include being a <a href="http://www.blogactive.com/2004/08/action-use-ed-kochs-work-email-and-ask.html">semi-closeted right-wing homosexual</a>.</p>
