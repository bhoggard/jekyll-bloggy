<p>As we move into the middle of August and Chelsea starts shutting down for the month, there are still some interesting things happening, especially related to performance.  There are 2 events tomorrow I recommend:</p>
<ul>
	<li>4pm: A walk-thru of the exhibition with private dealer Betsey Geffen, aka <a href="http://www.charleyfriedman.com/">Charley Friedman</a> as part of <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/4983"><em>Ceci n&#8217;est pas&#8230; (This is not&#8230;)</em></a> at Sara Meltzer Gallery</li>
	<li>All day: &quot;An improvisational dance performance by Felicia Ballos and Flora Wiegman. Staged in the midst of demolishing the exhibition format of Part One and constructing the setting for Part Two, the performance takes inspiration from the actions performed by the gallery workers, with each dancer creating her movements in response. &quot;  This is part of <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/5179"><em>Carte Blanche</em></a> at Elizabeth Dee Gallery.</li>
</ul>
