<p>I&#8217;m not sure I recognize this country anymore.  I think we&#8217;ve slipped down the <a href="http://www.doonesbury.com/strip/dailydose/index.cfm?uc_full_date=20030313&uc_comic=db&uc_daction=X">rabbit hole</a> and only a few people I know are still sane.</p>
<p>Americans say they&#8217;re <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/003254.html">ready to go after Iran and Syria</a> next, and that driving Hummers is patriotic.  They think war is something to watch on TV.  This is also war, more than driving some 10 mpg <span class="caps">SUV</span> is:</p>
<blockquote><img alt="marine-father.jpg" src="/mt/archives/marine-father.jpg" width="409" height="285" border="0" /><br />
<br />
William Buesing <span class="caps">III</span>, the biological father of U.S. Marine Lance Cpl. Brian Buesing, is overcome with grief as he holds the U.S. Flag presented to him at the funeral services for his son Saturday, April 5, 2003 at Cedar Key, Fla.</blockquote>
<p>The government can <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/003250.html">hold American citizens</a> in jail without charging them using sealed warrants.  We are not a nation of laws if no one bothers to <a href="http://www.talkleft.com/archives/002847.html">enforce the Constitution</a>, and frankly that&#8217;s one of the only good aspects of our society.  If we lose that, what do we get to be proud of?  A <a href="/2003/04/we-know-what-cu.html">commercial culture</a>?  The fact we can&#8217;t provide healthcare to over <a href="http://www.ama-assn.org/sci-pubs/amnews/pick_02/gvbf1216.htm">41 million</a> Americans?  That we make things like <a href="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B00008DDGU/tristanmedial-20">Something About Mary: The Collectors Edition</a>?</p>
