It&#8217;s in a cool little opera house-like theatre in the East Village.  Check out the review in this week&#8217;s Time Out NY.  I think it&#8217;s a better review than <a href="http://www.villagevoice.com/issues/0220/sellar.php">this one</a> in the Village Voice.</p>
