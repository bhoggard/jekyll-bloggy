<p>The website <a href="http://www.dailykos.com/">Daily Kos</a> is one of the most influential political blogs / communities on the &#8217;net.    Their second convention, called <a href="http://www.yearlykosconvention.org/">YearlyKos</a> will take place in Chicago August 2-5, 2007.</p>
<p>There is a <a href="http://www.dailykos.com/storyonly/2007/1/8/21220/25736">new way</a> for artists to get involved with this year&#8217;s convention.</p>
<blockquote>Calling all artists, poets, performers, musicians and art lovers at DailyKos to check a new Yahoo group, <a href="http://groups.yahoo.com/group/ArtKos/">ArtKos</a>, which has been formed to develop and plan some arts and politics related activities and events for this year&rsquo;s Ykos in Chicago. The idea was spawned recently in KingOneEye&rsquo;s diary <a href="http://www.dailykos.com/story/2007/1/3/134532/6877 ">Join the Art Insurgency</a> This groupsite is also a place to make connections, share art related political news, events, exhibitions, websites, and whatever strikes people&#8217;s interest, not to mention discussion and inspiration! &nbsp;This group and our plans are not officially affiliated with or endorsed by the Ykos organization, though they have been informed of our existence and our ideas.</blockquote>
