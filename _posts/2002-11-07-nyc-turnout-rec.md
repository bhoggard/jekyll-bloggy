<p>From the <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2002/11/07/nyregion/metrocampaigns/07TURN.html?ex=1037336400&en=1ea2bb57f848c43f&ei=5062&partner=GOOGLE">NY Times</a>:</p>
<blockquote>New York City registered what may be a record low in voter turnout on Tuesday, with an estimated 34.2 percent of registered voters casting their ballots. The last time city voters came out in similarly low numbers was in 1990, when 38 percent of registered voters voted. New York State may also have reached a new low with an estimated 40 percent of voters casting their ballots.</blockquote>
