<p>Yesterday, after our <a href="/2006/05/robin-foottit-a-1.html">visit</a> to The Bronx, we went to Rockefeller Center for <a href="http://www.clementine-gallery.com/artrock2006.html">Art Rock 2006</a>.  My favorite works were Noonan&#8217;s (Foxy Production), plus <a href="/2005/11/good-art-in-che.html">Larry Mantello</a> (<a href="http://www.kusteratiltongallery.com/">Kustera Tilton</a>) and <a href="http://www.tmpspace.com/">Brody Condon</a> (<a href="http://www.virgilgallery.com/v2/?ikDirId=1064">Virgil de Voldere</a>).</p>
<p>Click <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/bloggy/tags/artrock2006/">here</a> for a few of the photos I took.</p>
<p>We also managed to catch the final minutes of the <a href="http://disdressed.blogspot.com/2006/04/on-semi-skirt-related-note.html">Donald Judd &#8220;exhibit&#8221;</a> at Christie&#8217;s.  We thought we had missed it, then ran into <a href="http://www.damelioterras.com/artists_art.asp?art_code=58">Tony Feher</a> who gave us directions and told us to run over there.  I&#8217;m very glad we did.  It was exquisite.</p>
