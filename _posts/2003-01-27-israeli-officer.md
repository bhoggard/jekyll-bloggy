<p>An Israeli officer has been <a href="http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/middle_east/2698887.stm">removed from his post</a> for obstructing an air attack against the Palestinians.</p>
<p>According to the Israeli newspaper, Maariv, the military intelligence officer held back information &#8220;for reasons of conscience&#8221; because he believed the raid would harm civilians.</p>
