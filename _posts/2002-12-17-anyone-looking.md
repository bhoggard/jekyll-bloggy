<p>Cool fellowship from Hofstra:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.hofstra.edu/Academics/Law/law_lgbt_fellowships.cfm">Fellowships for the Advocacy for the Equality of Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual and Transgendered People</a></p>
<blockquote>Each year, the Law School will award fellowships to up to three(3) incoming J.D. students with a history of advocacy on behalf of the <span class="caps">LGBT</span> community.<br />
<br />
<span class="caps">PROGRAM</span> <span class="caps">OVERVIEW</span><br />
<br />
1.   A tuition fellowship of up to $20,000 for each year of law school.<br />
<br />
2.   A $5,000 stipend to support a summer externship related to <span class="caps">LGBT</span> advocacy.<br />
<br />
3.   A comprehensive course of study devoted to equality, including courses in Sexuality and the Law, Sex Discrimination, Jurisprudence, and an independent study and tutorial designed to address issues of particular concern to the <span class="caps">LGBT</span> community.<br />
<br />
4.   Experience in legal advocacy for the <span class="caps">LGBT</span> community through the Law School&#8217;s externship program which places students with nonprofit organizations, including those devoted to legal advocacy on behalf of the <span class="caps">LGBT</span> community.<br />
<br />
5.   Participation in a mentoring program with LeGal, the Lesbian and Gay Lawyers Association of Greater New York, representing one of the most diverse legal practice communities in the United States inclusive of <span class="caps">LGBT</span> individuals.</blockquote>
