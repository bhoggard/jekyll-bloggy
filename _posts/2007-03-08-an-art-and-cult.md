<p>Many art bloggers such as myself would like to be compensated a bit for our time, or at least our hosting costs.  In our case, our two blogs use up a lot of bandwidth, especially <a href="http://jameswagner.com/">James</a>&#8217;s.</p>
<p>I&#8217;m researching the possibility of setting up an art and culture ad network, so someone could come to one place and buy ads on various art and culture blogs, plus <a href="http://www.artcal.net/">ArtCal</a> if they so choose.  It also would give those of us who want ads, but don&#8217;t want hyper-ugly animated ones, a chance I hope.</p>
<p>If you&#8217;re interested in such a thing, either as a publisher/blogger, or as a potential advertiser, drop me an <a href="mailto:barry@tristanmedia.com">email</a>.  Feel free to comment here as well if you have any ideas.</p>
