<p>I only had my iPhone with me, so these aren&#8217;t the greatest photos.  This guy was in the West 4th Street subway.  The top was removed to give more volume, and it sounded like a honky-tonk piano, but his playing was quite good, ranging from &#8220;Hey Jude&#8221; to Chopin.  No one else was with him so I don&#8217;t know if he managed to get the piano down there via the station elevator by himself.  Having just been to a great <a href="http://jameswagner.com/2009/02/the_human_voice_in_a.html">concert</a> of experimental music, this was a nice subway surprise.</p>
<p><strong>Update:</strong> Olympia Lambert already <a href="http://olysmusings.blogspot.com/2009/02/colin-huggins-new-street-art.html">posted</a> about him in early February.  He is <a href="http://www.thecrazypianoguy.com/">Colin Huggins</a> (aka The Crazy Piano Guy).  He is also the music director of the Joffrey Ballet!</p>
