<p>Via <a href="http://crainsny.com/news.cms?newsId=8752">Crain&#8217;s New York</a>:</p>
<blockquote> Broadway was hit hard last week when the Republican National Convention was in town.<br />
<br />
Attendance for the week ending Sept. 5 dropped 18% to 163,977 compared with the same week last year. Box-office grosses plummeted 20%, to $10.9 million, despite the League of American Theatres and Producers&#8217; &#8220;Unconventional Wisdom&#8221; discount ticket program. That promotion sold $215,000 worth of tickets.</blockquote>
