<p>Tyler Green <a href="http://www.artsjournal.com/man/archives20040201.shtml#68248">points out</a> an interesting fact:</p>
<blockquote>Matthew Barney&#8217;s The Cremaster Cycle wasn&#8217;t the Krensian success that the Guggenheim (and a compliant press corps) had claimed. Here&#8217;s why: Cremaster drew 3,151 visitors a day to the GuggEnron. Meanwhile, the show immediately after Cremaster drew 3,314 visitors a da, outdrawing Cremaster by over 100 people a day. What outdrew Cremaster? A permanent collection hanging.</blockquote>
