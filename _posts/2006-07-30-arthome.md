<p>Via <a href="http://www.culturebot.org/archives/2006/07/27/ArtHomeOnline.php">Culturebot</a>  I just learned about <a href="http://www.arthomeonline.org/">ArtHome</a> which sounds like a good organization for artists to investigate.</p>
<blockquote>ArtHome helps artists build assets and equity through financial literacy and home-ownership.<br />
<br />
We foster long-term stability in the American Arts and Culture sector by harnessing the economic power of real estate and equity on behalf of individual culture workers.<br />
<br />
Through our dynamic financial literacy and home-buying curriculum, and through the creation of home buying programs, access to innovative loan products and down payment assistance, we are building a unique and complementary support structure for American culture.</blockquote>
<p>The founders are the theater artist Aaron Landsman and Esther Robinson of <a href="http://www.creative-capital.org/">Creative Capital</a> fame.</p>
