<p>Empty Studio, 70 Washington Street, <span class="caps">DUMBO</span></p>
<blockquote>The area under the Manhattan Bridge overpass is being condominium-ized, and art spaces are on the move. Smack Mellon will soon leave its fragrant gallery in an old spice factory for other quarters. The many studios in 70 Washington Street are emptying out. During this transition, an arts group called <span class="caps">TAG</span> Projects, run by Derick Melander, Tim Kent and John Silvis, is using one vacated space for a very short-run exhibition, with Peter Corrie as curator.</blockquote>
&#8212; From Holland Cotter&#8217;s <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2004/06/25/arts/design/25GALL.html?ex=1088827200&en=87be953076b3bfac&ei=5062&partner=GOOGLE">review</a> of &#8216;Death to the Fascist Insect That Preys on the Life of the People&#8217;
<p>The show, which we saw today (its last day) was great.  Watch <a href="http://www.tagprojects.com/"><span class="caps">TAG</span> Projects</a> for future cool things.  I think James will be writing up something soon.</p>
