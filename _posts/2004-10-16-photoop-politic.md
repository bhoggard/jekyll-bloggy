<p>Go see Photo-Op, by Conrad Cummings, <a href="http://www.avianmusic.com/upcoming.html#Politics">next week</a>.  It&#8217;s being presented on October 28-29.  <a href="/2004/08/conrad-cummings.html">We went</a> to a performance with excerpts, but the whole thing is being done on those days.  For the visual arts crowd, your reason to see it:  James Siena wrote the libretto.</p>
<p>Here is a <a href="http://www.newmusicbox.org/webcasts/po/index.nmbx">video</a> from the performance we attended.  I recommend going to 21.5 minutes in to hear &#8220;By keeping things exactly the way that they are&#8230;&#8221; and 29 minutes in to the Purcell-meets-Gilbert-and-Sullivan aria &#8220;Would you die for me?&#8221;</p>
