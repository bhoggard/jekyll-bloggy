<p>James and I visited this <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/3921">exhibition</a> of <span class="caps">BFA</span> students&#8217; art at the SVA&#8217;s 26th Street gallery on Saturday.  My favorite pieces in the show were in the project room  with print-related work curated by Gunars Prande, especially those by <a href="http://mattlifson.com/">Matt Lifson</a> and Lauren Baez.</p>
