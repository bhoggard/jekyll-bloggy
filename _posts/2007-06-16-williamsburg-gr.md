<p>The page on ArtCal formerly know as &#8220;Williamsburg / Greenpoint&#8221; is now <a href="http://www.artcal.net/allevents/2">Williamsburg / Greenpoint / Bushwick</a>, as the relentless march of gentrification means that many of the galleries that are interesting to James and me are increasingly distant from the Bedford and Lorimer stops.  This doesn&#8217;t mean we don&#8217;t still have some favorites near those stops.</p>
<p>Here is an example: Brooklyn Fireproof now has an additional space in Bushwick called Temporary Storage that has a very interesting-looking <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/4972">show</a> opening June 23rd.</p>
