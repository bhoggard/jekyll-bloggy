<p>Foreign Policy has a very interesting <a href="http://www.foreignpolicy.com/issue_julyaug_2002/wallerstein.html">article</a> whose thesis is that America is now in decline because of a number of factors, including:<br />
<ul><br />
<li>Public and private spending that is too heavily focused on military technology</li><br />
<li>The damage to our economic interests from an attack on Iraq</li><br />
<li>The likely failure of an attack on Iraq to &#8220;win&#8221; given the high risk of casualties and the lack of public support for combat leading to significant losses</li><br />
<li>The collapse in our world stature and leadership when we, as the foremost military power in history, fail to create a sustainable regime to follow Saddam Hussein</li></p>
</ul>
