<p>On this date in 2004, <a href="http://jameswagner.com/">James</a> and I launched the first version of the <a href="http://www.artcat.com/">ArtCat Calendar</a> (then called ArtCal).  The first version just listed shows and dates by neighborhood, with the main innovation being the listing of Chelsea shows by street.</p>
<p>Some history:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Images added: September 6, 2005</li>
	<li><span class="caps">RSS</span> and iCal feeds added: December 12, 2005</li>
	<li>E-mail newsletter launched: March 30, 2006</li>
	<li>Newsletter reaches 1000 subscribers: March 13, 2007</li>
	<li>Redesigned: August 28, 2007</li>
	<li>Merged ArtCal and ArtCat: March 2009</li>
</ul>
