<p>My cold is back, and I&#8217;m busy, so no brilliant write-up for you, just a recommendation.  Go see <a href="http://www.targetmargin.org/ticket.html">These Very Serious Jokes</a>, the beginning of Target Margin&#8217;s Faust project.  They are doing their own translation, by Douglas Langworthy, and it is beautiful.  Plus: <a href="http://www.playbill.com/news/article/83077.html">David Greenspan</a> plays Mephistopheles!</p>
<p>The run (at <a href="http://www.here.org"><span class="caps">HERE</span></a>) ends February 1:</p>
<p>Sun Jan 25 @ 7pm<br />
Tues Jan 27 &#8211; Sat Jan 31 @ 8.30pm<br />
Sat Jan 31 and Sun Feb 1 @ 4pm</p>
<p>Target Margin and The Civilians (mentioned a couple of days ago) are two of the most interesting theater groups out there.  I go see almost everything they do (exceptions only for scheduling, not aesthetic reasons) &#8212; in the case of TM since 1991!</p>
