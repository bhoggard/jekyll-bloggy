<p>We have found ourselves in Williamsburg several times over the last month.  My current picks for favorite shows there:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Beth Bridea &amp; Tucker Nichols at <a href="http://www.southfirst.org/PR%20Everything%20Beautiful.htm"><span class="caps">SOUTHFIRST</span></a> &#8211; Tucker is also in the <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/004366.html">current show</a> at The Drawing Center</li>
	<li>Janice Caswell at <a href="http://www.schroederromero.com/">Schroeder Romero</a> &#8211; beautiful use of pins, wall painting, and other media.  I had seen her works on paper before, but they hadn&#8217;t made me realize what she is capable of in a whole room installation.</li>
	<li><em>No Return</em> at <a href="http://www.momentaart.org/cur_pro/noreturn.html">Momenta</a> &#8211; a number of good works that come together as a brilliant group show.</li>
</ul>
<p>I am expecting some images from Schroeder Romero of a young artist who has a solo show coming up.  I will write about that later.</p>
