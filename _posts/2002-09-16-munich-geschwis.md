<p>We visited the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geschwister-Scholl-Platz">Geschwister Scholl</a> Platz today.  They were a brother and sister who were killed by the Nazis for distributing anti-Nazi leaflets at the Ludwig Maximilian University.  The memorial is very good &#8212; there are ceramic versions of the leaflets on the pavement near the windows from which they were thrown.</p>
