<p>Wow &#8211; at the rate the world is moving, the <span class="caps">USA</span> will have more in common with autocracies/theocracies in the Middle East than the rest of the West:</p>
<p><a href="http://news.bbc.co.uk/hi/english/uk/newsid_1963000/1963522.stm">Police back softer line on drug users</a></p>
<blockquote>Police chiefs say they would have a better chance of winning the war on drugs if addicts were given treatment instead of punishment.<br />
<br />
The Association of Chief Police Officers (<span class="caps">ACPO</span>) also believes it would be better to adopt a more relaxed stance towards people caught with small amounts of cannabis.<br />
<br />
<span class="caps">ACPO</span> unveiled its proposals in a report, carried out by its influential drugs committee, saying in some circumstances, treatment should be considered instead of prosecution.</blockquote>
