<p>In an <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2002/10/31/politics/campaigns/31JANE.html?pagewanted=all&position=top">article</a> on the fact that the wife of Arkansas&#8217;s governor, Mike Huckabee, is running for Secretary of State at the same time he is running for re-election against my close friend <a href="http://www.jimmieloufisher.com/">Jimmie Lou Fisher</a>, we have a number of nice quotes.  She (the wife) is so embarassing.</p>
<blockquote> Mr. Huckabee was already being accused of having pretensions to royalty. For example, he accepted $23,000 in clothing and gift certificates from Jennings Osborne, a wealthy supporter and appointee, in 2000 alone, then sued to block the state ethics commission from investigating such gifts.<br />
<br />
But his wife&#8217;s campaign difficulties have added to the governor&#8217;s burden. Her insistence on her own 24-hour state police detail, her crisscrossing the state to campaign events in a giant trooper-driven Ford Excursion provided by the state and her travel in and out of Arkansas on the state airplane, both with and without her husband, for reasons the governor&#8217;s office refuses to disclose all produced a month&#8217;s worth of bad press for both Huckabees at the start of the fall campaign.<br />
<br />
&#8230;<br />
<br />
 Mrs. Huckabee remains indignant, writing off her detractors as either mistaken about her or mean-spirited. &#8220;I&#8217;d be lying if I said it didn&#8217;t bother me,&#8221; she said in an interview. &#8220;If it wasn&#8217;t for the grace of God, I&#8217;d have shot a few people already.&#8221;<br />
<br />
&#8220;Jesus wasn&#8217;t liked, either,&#8221; she added. &#8220;And Jesus was mistreated, and called names.&#8221;<br />
<br />
Democrats say Mrs. Huckabee has shown a mean streak of her own, particularly in her only televised debate with Mr. Daniels, on Oct. 16. There, she raised the subject of Mr. Daniels&#8217;s drunken-driving convictions in 1983 and 1990, the more recent offense in a state vehicle; attacked him for putting his wife and daughter on the state payroll; and said he had not come up with &#8220;an original idea the whole campaign.&#8221;<br />
<br />
Mr. Daniels&#8217;s driving-while-intoxicated record was well known, and he apologized again for it. But Mrs. Huckabee maintains that he would be a bad role model for Arkansas youths. &#8220;He says his problem with alcohol was 12 years ago, and I know that&#8217;s not true,&#8221; she said this week. &#8220;I&#8217;ve had personal experience with it, but I haven&#8217;t brought that out. He could hardly stand up at the governor&#8217;s gala last Christmas.&#8221;<br />
<br />
&#8230;<br />
<br />
 She has a daredevil streak. As a youngster, she crossed the Arkansas River on a pipeline in the black of night. As first lady, she has gone bungee jumping, and even skydiving Â— albeit strapped to an Army paratrooper.</blockquote>
<p>Well, maybe I miss it a little.  You have to read her statements in the proper accent.  Also, where do I sign up to get strapped to an Army paratrooper?</p>
