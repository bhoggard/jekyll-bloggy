<p>bloggy.com can report that <a href="http://www.dcktcontemporary.com/"><span class="caps">DCKT</span> Contemporary</a> will reopen at its new location on the Lower East Side, just east of <a href="http://www.jenbekman.com/">Jen Bekman</a> and just south of the <a href="http://www.newmuseum.org/">New Museum</a>, around the 3rd week of March.  The first show will be a solo exhibition by <a href="http://joshazzarella.com/">Josh Azzarella</a>, his first in <span class="caps">NYC</span>.</p>
