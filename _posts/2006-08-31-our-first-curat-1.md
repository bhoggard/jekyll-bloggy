<p><a href="http://jameswagner.com">James</a> and I are curating a pair of shows at <a href="http://www.damstuhltrager.com/">Dam, Stuhltrager</a>.  The first is a sculpture show in their backyard sculpture garden, by <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/005639.html">Susan C. Dessel</a>.  It opens September 8th.  <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/2969">ArtCal</a> has the details.</p>
<p>The second part opens on Friday, October 13th.  This will be a group show in two of the gallery&#8217;s rooms.  I&#8217;ll post more details once we decide on a name and have finalized the list of artists.  The theme you ask?  If you know us or read or blogs, you&#8217;ll know we have trouble limiting ourselves to one genre or idea.  Look for something dealing with our various interests: politics, conceptual art, humor, and beauty.</p>
