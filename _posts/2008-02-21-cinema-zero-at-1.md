Filmmaker Amy Granat teams up with choreographers Felicia Ballos and Flora Wiegmann to present a new collaborative film and dance performance. In addition, Granat has selected a film and video program featuring short works by Hollis Frampton, Joan Jonas, Peter Kubelka, Richard Serra, and Joyce Wieland, among others.<br />

Cinema Zero is an ongoing project that activates connections between artists of different generations and fosters experimentation across disciplines.</blockquote>
