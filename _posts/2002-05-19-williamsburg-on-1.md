<p>We spent the afternoon walking around Williamsburg, going to a few galleries.  The <a href="http://priskajuschkafineart.net/hawkins/ep.html">Stuart Hawkins</a> show at Priska Juschka is grrreeat!  The Thomas Lail show at <a href="http://www.goliath777.com/current.html">Goliath</a> is cool too.  Schroeder Romero had an amusing neon piece in the back by Bill Rowe (who teaches at <a href="http://www.clt.astate.edu/wrowe/"><span class="caps">ASU</span></a>, near my home town!), called &#8220;Who&#8217;s your daddy?&#8221;.</p>
<p>There appears to be a new Italian (Sardinian to be precise) wine bar called <span class="caps">DOC</span> at 7th and Wythe, open every day from 6-midnight or later.  I expect to try it out soon.</p>
<p>Late in the afternoon we sat outside at Yabby, had coffee, and watched the pigeons.</p>
