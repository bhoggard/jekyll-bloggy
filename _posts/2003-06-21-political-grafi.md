<blockquote>In the coming months a black spot will pop up everywhere&#8230; on store windows and newspaper boxes, on gas pumps and supermarket shelves. Open a magazine or newspaper &#8211; it&#8217;s there. It&#8217;s on TV. It stains the logos and smears the nerve centers of the world&#8217;s biggest corporations.<br />
                                                                 <br />
This is the mark of the people who don&#8217;t approve of Bush&#8217;s plan to control the world, who don&#8217;t want countries &#8220;liberated&#8221; without UN backing, who can&#8217;t stand anymore neo-con bravado shoved down their throats.<br />
                                                                 <br />
This is the mark of the people who want the Kyoto Protocol for the environment, who want the International Criminal Court for greater justice, who want a world where all nations, including the U.S.A., are free of weapons of mass destruction, and who pledge to take their country back.</blockquote>
