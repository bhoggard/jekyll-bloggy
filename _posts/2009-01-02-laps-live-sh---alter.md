<p>This looks like a great weekend of performance, curated by Chase Granoff and Chris Peck:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.chocolatefactorytheater.org/e_laps.html"><span class="caps">LAPS</span>: Live Sh&#8212; Alternative Presenter Fair</a></p>
<p>Saturday January 10 6-10pm &amp; Sunday January 11 4-8pm</p>
<p><span class="caps">FREE</span> admission, homemade chocolate chip cookies and cheap drinks!</p>
<p><a href="http://www.chocolatefactorytheater.org/home.html">The Chocolate Factory Basement</a><br />
5-49 49th Avenue<br />
L.I.C., NY 11101<br />
<a href="http://www.chocolatefactorytheater.org/contact.html">Directions</a></p>
<p>Live Sh&#8212; invites a number of independent presenters (including performance series, record labels, galleries, and collectives) to share video and documentation from their associated artists in a casual fair format. The event will include informal discussions with presenters from around the country, including institutionally-affiliated guests. <span class="caps">LAPS</span> is a direct response and alternative to <span class="caps">APAP</span> (Association of Performing Arts Presenters).</p>
