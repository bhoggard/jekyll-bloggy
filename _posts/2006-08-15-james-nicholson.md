<p>I just received email that <a href="http://www.nicholsongallery.com/">James Nicholson Gallery</a> has closed.  I&#8217;m glad I saw their last <a href="/2006/07/chelsea-highlig.html">group show</a>.</p>
<p>That&#8217;s the bad news.  The good news is that their space is being taken over by two interesting galleries out of Frankfurt, <a href="http://www.galerieadler.com/">Galerie Adler</a> and <a href="http://www.galerie-poller.com/">Galerie Poller</a>.  I can&#8217;t wait to see shows from those two here in Chelsea.</p>
