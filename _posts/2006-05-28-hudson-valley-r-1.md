<p>James, Mom, and I rented a <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/bloggy/tags/minicooper/">Mini Cooper convertible</a> from <a href="http://www.zipcar.com/">Zipcar</a> on Friday and took a road trip to the Hudson Valley, including Cold Spring, Wappinger Falls, and New Paltz.  I highly recommend seeing <a href="http://www.hhs-newpaltz.org/">Huguenot Street</a> in New Paltz, with houses dating from the early 1700s.  I can also recommend the <a href="http://www.hvgateway.com/FOUNDRY.HTM">Foundry Cafe</a> in Cold Spring for lunch, and the Ground Hog in Wappinger Falls for coffee or a meal.</p>
