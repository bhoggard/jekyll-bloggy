<p>You can use the search box on my site, or on James&#8217;s, to see how often we  have written about <a href="http://www.ericdoeringer.com/">Eric Doeringer</a>.  We are big fans.</p>
<p>On Saturday, <a href="http://www.mikeweissgallery.com/">Mike Weiss</a> called the police on Eric.  James has the <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/005203.html">story</a>.  This is my favorite part:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>He packed up his work and confronted Mr. Weiss, who admitted he had called the police. He said that he didn&#8217;t like &#8220;seeing people walking around with tiny paintings,&#8221; while he was paying high rent for his gallery and, &#8220;trying to sell $30,000 paintings.&#8221;</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>When Doeringer told him he was certainly going to let everyone he knew in the art world hear about what he had done, Weiss said, &#8220;If that&#8217;s the way you want to play it, I&#8217;ll call the police whenever I see you anywhere.&#8221;</p>
</blockquote>
<p>I think 24th Street needs more of art like Eric&#8217;s not less.</p>
