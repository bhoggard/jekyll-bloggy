<p>This amazing <strike>antique</strike> carousel was one of two set up in plazas in Segovia the day we visited.  The golden stone you see behind it is the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aqueduct_of_Segovia">Roman aqueduct</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Update:</strong>  James said he didn&#8217;t think it was really antique, and he is correct.  It&#8217;s made to look that way, but the two we saw were <a href="http://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Le_Man%C3%A9ge_d%27Andrea">created</a> by la <span class="caps">MACHINE</span> in Toulousse, France in 1999.</p>
