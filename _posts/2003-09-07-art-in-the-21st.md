<p>After years of never watching TV, I&#8217;m still getting used to having a <a href="/2003/08/if-you-dont-hav.html">digital recorder</a>, and looking to see what&#8217;s on TV that I might actually want to see.</p>
<p>As a visual person, I look at <a href="http://story.news.yahoo.com/news?tmpl=index&cid=705">Yahoo! Top Stories Photos</a> to see what they&#8217;re showing.  I was amazed to see a photo of Kiki Smith on the page.  It was to illustrate the TV show <a href="http://www.pbs.org/art21/">Art in the 21st Century</a> on <span class="caps">PBS</span>.  How have I not heard of this?!  A show that talks to the likes of her, Paul Pfeiffer, Collier Schorr, etc. is definitely something I want to see.  The web page gives air dates, so set those recorders.</p>
<p>I saw Kiki Smith in person at <span class="caps">LFL</span> Gallery when I was there for <a href="http://www.lflgallery.com/phelps.html">Danica Phelps</a>&#8217;s opening on Friday night.</p>
