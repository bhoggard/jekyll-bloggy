<p><a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/3828">This</a> was one of my favorite shows in December.  It&#8217;s closed now, but here is a slideshow with some not-so-great images of Kate Levant&#8217;s work along with some installation shots.  We met Kate when she came to Dam Stuhltrager to help <a href="http://danglingbetween.com/artists/jacques_louis_vidal/">Jacques Louis Vidal</a> install his big lightbox for our <a href="http://danglingbetween.com/">show</a>.  The one up in the ceiling by the fluorescent light had a sound component too.</p>
<p>The other artists in the show were Brian Clifton, Jeffrey P. Porterfield, Joshua Smith, Mike Smith and Ned Vena.</p>
