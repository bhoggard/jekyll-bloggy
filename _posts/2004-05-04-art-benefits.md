<p>Good things come to those who go to art benefits.  The <a href="http://www.momentaart.org/cur_pro/benefit1_04.html">Momenta benefit</a> operates as a raffle &#8212; you pick a work among those remaining when your name is called.  This time we had three tickets, and one of those was drawn first.  We chose the <a href="http://www.moma.org/exhibitions/2003/kikismith/">Kiki Smith</a> work pictured above.  We also got a painting by Joey Kotting and a mixed media/collage work by <a href="http://www.michaelcambre.com/">Michael Cambre</a>.  It was one of our best experiences ever at such an event.</p>
<p>Tomorrow (Wednesday May 5) we&#8217;re going to <a href="http://www.groundswellmural.org/">Groundswell</a>&#8216;s benefit.  Tickets are $100 or 3 for $250 for the art raffle, or a minimum of $15 to go to the party.  You get to a good party with a cool art crowd, and it&#8217;s a good cause.  Groundswell brings artists and members of a given <span class="caps">NYC</span> community together to create a permanent mural in their neighborhood.  More info on them is <a href="http://www.groundswellmural.org/">here</a>.</p>
