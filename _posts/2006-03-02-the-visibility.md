<p>I forgot to mention one thing that surprised me about the press remarks at the Whitney Biennial <a href="/2006/03/whitney-biennia.html">press preview</a>.</p>
<p>The only person who spoke to the press other than Adam Weinberg and the two curators was Jennifer P. Goodale, Vice President, Contributions, Altria Corporate Services, Inc.  I realize Altria is an important funder of the arts, and I really appreciate how much money (however earned) is given by them to so many cultural organizations.  However, it struck an odd note for me.  I also see that the <a href="http://www.whitney.org/www/exhibition/biennial_release.jsp">press release</a> has a quote from Ms. Goodale as the third paragraph.</p>
