<p>Yes, it&#8217;s 11MB, but it&#8217;s worth it!  This work from 1942 was composed for <a href="http://www.merce.org/">Merce Cunningham</a> (Cage&#8217;s partner until the end of his life). People like <a href="http://www.newalbion.com/artists/cagej/">Cage</a> and Cunningham created the <span class="caps">NYC</span> that we love, as opposed to the parts we don&#8217;t.  Imagine &#8220;sampling&#8221; a Shostokovich symphony in a composition for dance during <span class="caps">WWII</span>!</p>
