<p>Mindy Tucker</p>

<p>The artist&#8217;s description of the project:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>The Lance Project consists of images of young men who resemble in some small way my friend Lance. The images constitute a composite portrait of him. Though I knew Lance for eight years, he died without my ever having his photograph.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>The rest of it is <a href="http://www.flaneur.org/04_02/0402_tucker/index.html">here</a>, and the artist has a <a href="http://www.withreservation.com/">web site</a> too.</p>
<p>I found this moving and beautiful project via John Morris, who runs the <a href="http://www.diggingpitt.com/">Digging Pitt</a> gallery in Pittsburgh.  I first heard of him and the gallery via the artist (and Pittsburgh native ) Emily Noelle Lambert.</p>
