<p>Next weekend, the <a href="http://www.chelseasymphony.org/">Chelsea Symphony</a> (we think they&#8217;re <a href="http://jameswagner.com/2007/09/chelsea_symphon_1.html">awesome</a>) is honoring artist <a href="http://www.cheimread.com/artists/louise-fishman/">Louise Fishman</a> for her 70th birthday.  The <a href="http://www.chelseasymphony.org/concerts/TCS_series4_2009.html">program</a> includes a new work by Aaron Dai based on a text by poet and former presidential candidate <a href="http://www.eileenmyles.com/">Eileen Myles</a> inspired by Louise&#8217;s <a href="http://jameswagner.com/2008/07/louise_fishman_angry.html">&#8220;Angry Paintings&#8221;</a>.</p>
