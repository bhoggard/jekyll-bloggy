<p>Doesn&#8217;t anyone care what it feels like to people in Manhattan when a commercial jet flies low <a href="http://1010wins.com/topstories/winstopstories_story_135065535.html">over lower Manhattan</a>?  We&#8217;re becoming so militarized that we&#8217;ll do anything for the benefit of the troops without thinking about how it affects anyone else.</p>
<blockquote>A commercial plane carrying returning military personnel flew near the Statue of Liberty and low over midtown Manhattan, prompting emergency calls from some concerned onlookers.<br />
<br />
The Continental Airlines plane, a Boeing 777, was given permission to change its flight plan as it approached its destination of Newark Liberty International Airport at about 8:30 a.m. Wednesday, the Federal Aviation Administration said.<br />
<br />
The flight pattern was intended &#8216;&#8217;to provide a special homecoming for members of our armed forces,&#8217;&#8217; the <span class="caps">FAA</span> said in a statement. </blockquote>
