<p>For those of you <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/006364.html">following along</a>, I&#8217;m wondering if the powers that be are realizing that appearing to be hostile to parades, especially queer ones at this time of year, is a bad thing.  According to <a href="http://www.onnyturf.com/articles/read.php?article_id=583">onNYTurf</a>, The Audre Lorde Project is getting their permit to parade in the streets on Friday after having been denied several times.  They received their permit as they were about to take the <span class="caps">NYPD</span> to court.</p>
<p>It will be interesting to see how &#8220;un-permitted&#8221; and historic protest marches such as the <a href="http://www.nycdykemarch.org/">Dyke March</a> and the <a href="http://www.theoccasionalfag.com/index.php?post_id=227489">Drag March</a> are treated.</p>
