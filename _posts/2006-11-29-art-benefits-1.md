<p><a href="http://www.wagmag.org/">The Second Annual <span class="caps">WAGMAG</span> Benefit Art Show</a></p>
<p>Admission is free, Tickets for artwork drawing are $150<br />
Tickets can be purchased at the gallery or online</p>
<p>Advanced Viewing: December 2nd-December 16th<br />
Reception and Raffle of work Saturday Dec. 16th, 7-9<br />
Viewing hours: Fri-Sun 1-6</p>
<p><span class="caps">WAGMAG</span> (Williamsburg and Greenpoint Monthly Art Guide) is pleased to present it&#8217;s Second annual benefit to support it&#8217;s mission to promote the over fifty galleries throughout Williamsburg and Greenpoint, to enlighten gallery goers as to art events and give recognition to the exhibiting artists through current listings and reviews.</p>
<p>All galleries included in <span class="caps">WAGMAG</span> are have been invited to participate in this exhibition, as well as some prominent artists from the neighborhood. The resulting show will not only generate funding for <span class="caps">WAGMAG</span> but also reflect the spirit and personality of the Williamsburg art community.</p>
<p>Works will be on view from Saturday, December 2nd through Saturday, December 16th. Tickets available during viewing and on the benefit evening. A ticket for $150 guarantees a work of art, a drawing decides the order in which ticketholders select an artwork. Benefit evening, Saturday December 16th 7-9, drawing begins at 8:00.</p>
