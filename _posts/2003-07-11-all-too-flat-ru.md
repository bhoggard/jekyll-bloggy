<p>Am I the last person to hear about <a href="http://www.alltooflat.com/">All Too Flat</a> &#8212; the geeky humor site?  I heard about their Rubik&#8217;s Cube stunt via <a href="http://travelersdiagram.com/archive/2003_07_06_archive.html#105785332531694948">Travelers Diagram</a>.</p>
<p>They <a href="http://www.alltooflat.com/pranks/cube/">transformed</a> the Tony Rosenthal sculpture at Astor Place into a Rubik&#8217;s Cube, via safe materials that wouldn&#8217;t harm it.   Plus, they provide cute pictures of themselves as they do it.  I&#8217;m reminded a bit of the nephews &#8212; the sons of <a href="http://jameswagner.com">James</a>&#8217;s brother.</p>
<p>I think my favorite member is <a href="http://www.alltooflat.com/pranks/dara/">Kenny B</a> &#8212; check out the <a href="http://www.alltooflat.com/about/personal/kennyb/kennyblog/">blog</a>.  They also provide a page of <a href="http://www.alltooflat.com/geeky/ggw/" title="We put the titty back in http://">Geeks Gone Wild</a> at Mardi Gras.</p>
