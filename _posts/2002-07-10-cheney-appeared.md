<blockquote>&#8220;I get good advice, if you will, from their people based upon how we&#8217;re doing business and how we&#8217;re operating over and above the just sort of normal by-the-book auditing arrangement,&#8221; Mr Cheney says in a short section of the video. </blockquote></p>
