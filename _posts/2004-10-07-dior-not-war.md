<p><a href="/2003/02/dissent-is-hot.html">We marched</a> with the GLAMericans for Peace in the February 15, 2003 anti-war march.  One of the slogans we chanted was &#8220;Dior Not War!&#8221;</p>
<p>Apparently John Galliano heard about that, or the phrase was born independently around the world in the last year and a half.</p>
