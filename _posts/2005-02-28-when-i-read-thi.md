<blockquote>To the Editor:<br />
<br />
Paul Krugman (&#8220;Kansas on My Mind,&#8221; column, Feb. 25) mentions that at a town hall meeting, college Republicans started chanting, &#8220;Hey hey, ho ho, Social Security&#8217;s got to go!&#8221; Well, at least they are being honest about their real aim, scrapping Social Security.<br />
<br />
Still, I can&#8217;t help but be furious thinking about this. We have a deadly, very expensive war going on. The dollar is sliding. Education needs a lot of work. But all these students can think of is that they want to scrap Social Security. And they seem happy about it. Don&#8217;t they have any shame? Don&#8217;t they have grandparents? Don&#8217;t they care about the elderly?<br />
<br />
This has got to be the coldest, most callous thing I have ever seen.<br />
<br />
Aaron Dellutri<br />
Chicago, Feb. 25, 2005</blockquote>
