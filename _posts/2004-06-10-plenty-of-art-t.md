<p>Things to attend:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.lehmannmaupin.com/">Lehmann Maupin</a>&#8217;s summer show, titled &#8220;Sommer Show&#8221;, features work from <a href="http://www.sommergallery.com/">Sommer Contemporary Art</a> in Tel Aviv.  (Get it?)  We met Irit Mayer-Sommer a couple of years ago at the Armory Show, and she shows some very interesting work (including a Bedouin photographer named Ahlam Shibil).  We have several <a href="http://www.nrw-kultursekretariat.de/programm/sha.htm">Gil Shani</a> works, both photography and drawing, we bought from her.</p>
<p>After that we&#8217;ll head to <span class="caps">DUMBO</span>, for <a href="http://www.smackmellon.org/">Smack Mellon</a>&#8217;s Open Studios from 6-9.  There is also a group show curated by Amanda Church and Courtney J. Martin up now.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.triangleworkshop.org/">Triangle Studios</a> has an open studio reception and benefit silent auction from 6-10 tonight.  There are some good names in the silent auction, including <a href="http://www.plusultragallery.com/exhibits/2003/andyyoder/index.html">Andy Yoder</a> and <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/004059.html">David Humphrey</a>.</p>
