<p>B&#8217;Tselem, the Israeli rights group, has issued an informative <a href="http://www.btselem.org/English/Press_Releases/2002/020513.asp">report</a> on the settlements in the Occupied Territories &#8212; titled &#8220;Land Grab: Israel&#8217;s Settlement Policy in the West Bank&#8221;.<br />
<blockquote>The research reveals that while the built-up areas of the settlements constitute only 1.7% of the land in the West Bank, the municipal boundaries are over three times as large: 6.8%. Regional councils constitute an additional 35.1%. Thus, a total of 41.9% of the area in the West Bank is controlled by the settlements.</blockquote><br />
As <a href="http://www.btselem.org/English/Publications/Summaries/Land_Grab_Map.asp">this map</a> shows, Palestinian communities have become settlements in an Israeli West Bank.</p>
