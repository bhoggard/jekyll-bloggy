<p>Talk about defining deviancy down!  Palestinian suicide bombers have nail bombs, Israel has <a href="http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/middle_east/2225706.stm">dart bombs</a>.</p>
<blockquote> Palestinian officials have condemned Israel&#8217;s reported use of tank shells which are said to have sprayed thousands of metal darts at a target in Gaza, killing four Palestinian civilians.<br />
<br />
Israeli media said the Israeli army confirmed it used flechettes in an operation near the Jewish settlement of Netzarim, south of Gaza City, on Thursday.<br />
<br />
Palestinian medical officials said the victims were cut to shreds after being hit by the tiny metal darts, which are dispersed by a tank shell.</blockquote>
