<p>James and I went to the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Irish_Hunger_Memorial">Irish Hunger Memorial</a> today to see an excerpt of <em>The Voyage of Garbhglas</em> by <a href="http://www.christopherwilliamsdance.org/">Christopher Williams</a> courtesy of the <a href="http://www.lmcc.net/calendar/event/sitelines_christopher_williams_the_voyage_of_garbhglas_excerpt/">Lower Manhattan Cultural Council</a>.</p>
<p>The <a href="http://www.vimeo.com/13840446">video</a> above is a short segment of the 30-minute performance.  I&#8217;ve also added one photo, and you can see more in my <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/bloggy/sets/72157624516471665/">flickr set</a>.</p>
<p>Never miss a chance to see this choreographer&#8217;s work. His 3-hour work <em>The Golden Legend</em> at <a href="http://www.dancetheaterworkshop.org/williams">Dance Theater Workshop</a> was funny, moving, and brilliant.</p>
