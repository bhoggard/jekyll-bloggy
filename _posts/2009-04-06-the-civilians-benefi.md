<p>The Civilians are holding their annual <a href="http://thecivilians.org/benefit2009.html">performance and benefit party</a> on <b>Friday, April 17th</b> at Galapagos <span class="caps">DUMBO</span>.  Their current projects include an investigation of the monstrous Atlantic Yards development in Brooklyn and its effects on the surrounding communities.</p>
<blockquote>
<p>Please join us at the newly reopened Galapagos Art Space in <span class="caps">DUMBO</span> for a one-night-only performance created from your stories. After a season spent investigating community in Brooklyn and Colorado Springs, we turn our attention to you and invite you to participate in an original Civilians piece by sharing personal insights about what <span class="caps">HOME</span> means to you.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>They also have a <a href="http://www.cmarket.com/auction/AuctionHome.action?vhost=thecivilians">silent auction</a> page up for bidding.  Items include theater memberships and a <a href="http://www.cmarket.com/auction/item/Item.action?_sourcePage=%2Fitem%2FbrowseImage.jsp&amp;id=85462299">print</a> by <a href="http://www.mixedgreens.com/">Mixed Greens</a> artist Coke Wisdom O’Neal.</p>
