<p>If you find yourself in Seattle in early September, don&#8217;t miss <a href="http://www.williampowhida.com/&#39;s">William Powhida</a> opening at <a href="http://www.platformgallery.com/">Platform</a>, our favorite gallery in that city.  I hear there will be music, record swapping, and more.  To get prepared, check out Bad At Sports&#8217;s interview with him on their <a href="http://badatsports.com/2008/episode-155-william-powhida-pete/">podcast</a>.  He starts about 8 or 9 minutes in.  You can hear his side of the story on <a href="http://www.zachfeuer.com/">Zach Feuer</a> getting his <span class="caps">NYC</span> gallery (<a href="http://www.schroederromero.com/">Schroeder Romero</a>) kicked out of the <a href="http://www.newartdealers.org/"><span class="caps">NADA</span></a> Art Fair because of an artwork by William in which he put a hex on Zach.</p>
