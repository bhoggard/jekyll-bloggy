<p>I went to see John Adams&#8217;s <a href="http://www.bam.org/performances/ElNino.asp">El Ni&ntilde;o</a> at <span class="caps">BAM</span> tonight.  Ugh.  I think living in California has ruined his mind.  Given the current state of the world, the last thing I want to hear is a religious work about Mary and the birth of Jesus.  I live in a country whose president says he is a man of faith and God wants him to bomb Iraq to make us safe.  Soaking in the most anti-woman, anti-human aspects of Christianity isn&#8217;t something I want to make time for.  Let&#8217;s think about it:  the only woman suitable to bear Jesus is a virgin, so the only really holy woman in Christianity does something no human woman can do:  bear a child while remaining a virgin.</p>
<p>The music isn&#8217;t even particulary interesting, with a few exceptions, and I love a lot of Adams&#8217;s work &#8212; especially <a href="http://service.bfast.com/bfast/click?bfmid=2181&sourceid=40185847&bfpid=0075597917727&bfmtype=music">Nixon in China</a> and <a href="http://service.bfast.com/bfast/click?bfmid=2181&sourceid=40185847&bfpid=0075597928129&bfmtype=music">Death of Klinghoffer</a>.</p>
<p>For a better written take on the whole thing, go read <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/003223.html">James</a>.</p>
