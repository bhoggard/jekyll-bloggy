<p>Clockwise from top left: Ghost Squadron. For search and rescue; National Reconnaissance Office. Dragon is code for infrared imaging on advanced KH-11 satellites; Desert Prowler. May represent Groom Lake, Nev., a k a Area 51; Special Projects Office. Oversaw F-117A stealth fighter support; 4451st Test Squadron. Stealth fighters; 413th Flight Test Squadron. Possibly referring to simulated or real electronic threats against aircraft. [From the <em>NY Times</em>, image and caption provided to them by Trevor Paglen]</p>

<p>Set your recorders.  The artist <a href="http://www.paglen.com/">Trevor Paglen</a> will be on <a href="http://www.comedycentral.com/colbertreport/index.jhtml">The Colbert Report</a> Monday night to talk about his new book titled <a href="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/1933633328/tristanmedial-20"><em>I Could Tell You But Then You Would Have to be Destroyed by Me: Emblems from the Pentagon&#8217;s Black World</em></a>.  It&#8217;s an exploration of the patches and emblems created by various &#8220;secret&#8221; organizations funded by the confidential $32 billion portion of the Defense Department&#8217;s annual budget.</p>
<p>Visit this <em>New York Times</em> <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2008/04/01/science/01patc.html?_r=2&oref=slogin&oref=slogin">article</a> for more on the subject.</p>
