<p>We went to the <a href="http://www.whitecolumns.org/benefit02/gallery.html">White Columns</a> benefit auction on Saturday night.  We saw of number of Art World people there, notably <a href="http://www.artnet.com/ag/galleryhomepage.asp?gid=664&which=fine">Kathy Magnan</a> and, most fabulous of all, <a href="http://www.eastwest.nu/blog.shtml">Choire</a>.</p>
<p>After a number of glasses of wine, I got into the spirit of the thing, and managed to purchase several items&#8230;<br />
<ul><br />
<li><a href="http://www.thing.net/~janine/">Janine Gordon</a> &#8211; a photograph of an attractive young latino man, titled &#8220;Tight Butt&#8221;</li><br />
<li>Shawn Green &#8211; a postcard plus a book/cd-rom on motorcyclists</li><br />
<li><a href="http://www.spaces.org/archive/mower.htm">Aimee Mower</a> &#8211; a small painting made from cake decorating gels</li><br />
<li><a href="http://users.rcn.com/jfsjr.interport/">John F. Simon, Jr.</a> &#8211; a work on paper from a computer plotter of a computer chip</li><br />
<li><a href="http://www.artnet.com/ag/fineartthumbnails.asp?gid=739&cid=13928">Frank Webster</a> &#8211; a beautiful acrylic on canvas painting</li></p>
</ul>
