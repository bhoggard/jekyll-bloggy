watercolor on paper<br />

<p><img alt="simone-huelser.jpg" src="/mt/archives/simone-huelser.jpg" width="449" height="600" /></p>
<p>Simone Huelser<br />
<em>Untitled</em>, 2006<br />
Color copies, wheat paste<br />
Dimensions variable</p>
<p>This was in a group show at <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/2704">Hudson Franklin</a> titled <em>Best Played with a Straight Face</em>. According to the gallery, the artist photographs different buildings, digitally manipulates them, has color copies produced and then wheat pastes them to the wall to achieve the pattern she wants.</p>
