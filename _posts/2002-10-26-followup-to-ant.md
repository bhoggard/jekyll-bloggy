<p>Arianna got <a href="http://www.salon.com/news/col/huff/2002/10/23/suv_ad/index.html">a huge response</a> to her anti-<span class="caps">SUV</span> advertising proposal.  There is now a fund set up to pay for such ads on TV.</p>
<p>Salon has an <a href="http://www.salon.com/books/int/2002/10/24/suvs/">interview</a> with the author of the book &#8220;High and Mighty&#8221; about SUVs, in which he explains that they&#8217;re not only bad for the environment, they&#8217;re not even safer than other vehicles.</p>
<p>Even the Car Talk guys are starting to <a href="http://cartalk.cars.com/info/suv/">speak out against them</a>.</p>
