<p>I have never understood why so many homos seems to think Hillary Clinton is somehow on our side.  She takes our money and shows up and gay fundraisers, but that&#8217;s the extent of her &#8220;support&#8221;.  While running for the Senate, she said she <a href="http://www.espany.org/pride/clintonforsenatevoteguide.html">would have voted for</a> the Defense of Marriage Act (<span class="caps">DOMA</span>).</p>
<p>It has now been one week since Senator Santorum&#8217;s <a href="/2003/04/jews-for-hitler.html">remarks</a>, and as this <a href="http://www.gaycitynews.com/gcn218/hillarysstonysilence.html">article</a> in the Gay City News reminds us, she hasn&#8217;t managed to make any statement whatsoever.  As of today, there&#8217;s no statement on Mr. Santorum on her <a href="http://clinton.senate.gov/index.html">web site</a>, but she has found the time to announce legislation to establish &#8220;National Purple Heart Recognition Day&#8221; and praise Schweizer Aircraft Corporation and its support of our troops.</p>
<p><a href="http://schumer.senate.gov/">Chuck Schumer</a> took a week to come up with a statement, but there&#8217;s nothing on his web site.  He lives in Park Slope, so he has plenty of queer neighbors!</p>
<p>You can contact our illustrious senators here: <a href="http://clinton.senate.gov/email_form.html">Clinton</a> and <a href="http://schumer.senate.gov/webform.html">Schumer</a>.  Hillary doesn&#8217;t have &#8220;Gay and Lesbian issues&#8221; in the topic choices, but Schumer does.</p>
