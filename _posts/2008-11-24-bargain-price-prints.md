<p>Klaus von Nichtssagend Gallery has a <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/8376">beautiful show</a> of new prints by <a href="http://jameswagner.com/2006/12/fourth_estate_a_1.html">Forth Estate</a>.  The main works of the show, by artists Glen Baldridge, Ian Cooper, Alex Dodge, Angela Dufresne, Matt Keegan, Sara Greenberger Rafferty, Joseph Hart, Paul Pope, Phil Sanders, Molly Smith, Will Yackulic, and Kevin Zucker, range in price from $250-2000.  In addition, there are five lithographs printed by Phil Sanders, some with hand watercoloring, for only $100 each.  The edition size is 10.   I&#8217;ve put images above of those five.  The show is up until December 21.</p>
