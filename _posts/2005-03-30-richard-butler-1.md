<p>We saw one of Butler&#8217;s paintings when we visited Van Brunt at the <a href="/2005/03/diva-art-fair-1.html">DiVA Fair</a>.  There is now a show at the <a href="http://www.vanbruntgallery.com/butler.html">gallery</a>, located in Beacon, NY.  Does the name sound familiar?  Butler is better known as the founder of the Psychedelic Furs.  I was told by Carl Van Brunt that he went to art school, and decided to make a career in music after being told by a professor that only a handful of visual artists in England actually made a living with their art.</p>
<p>Sounds worth a day trip, especially for those of us who grew up with New Wave.</p>
