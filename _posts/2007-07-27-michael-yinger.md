<p>Michael Yinger<br />
<em>all i know is that i don&#8217;t know nothin&#8217;</em>, 2007<br />
mixed media<br />
approx. 10 &#215; 20 feet</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>This work is part of an <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/5001">exhibition</a> curated by gallery artist <a href="http://priskajuschkafineart.net/exhibitions.php?id=61">Ryan Schneider</a> closing today, so head on over!  There are a number of great installations in the show.  Michael Yinger is from Indiana, so note that he chose white trash to make up that state.  Priska told me she had asked him about the cigarette butts used for Washington state, as we both agreed it seemed an odd choice.  He said he just liked the way they looked, and Washington was a big state.</p>
