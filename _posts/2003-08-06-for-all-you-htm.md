<p>Very cool tool!  If you find yourself editing <span class="caps">HTML</span>, or just having trouble fixing formatting problems in your blog posts, you have to get <a href="http://www.mozilla.org">Mozilla</a> and installl the <a title="placenamehere.com | projects | pnhtoolbar" href="http://placenamehere.com/pnhtoolbar/">pnhtoolbar</a>.</p>
<p>It lets you do cool stuff like outline all of the block elements &#8212; &#8220;why is that indented there?!&#8221;, view cookies, disable style sheets, enable a different style sheet, etc.</p>
