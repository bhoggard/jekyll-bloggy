<p>When it&#8217;s tank shells (or F-16s) rather than suicide bombs that kill civilians, Americans seem to think it&#8217;s not terrorism.  Israel shelled people in a bedouin encampment last night, killing a family of four, and wounding others, claiming there was movement in an area near a settlement where Palestinians were forbidden.  When I heard about it on <span class="caps">NPR</span> this morning, they said it was within an olive grove.  I know from my friends in <a href="http://www.jewsagainsttheoccupation.org/">JAtO</a> that now is the time for harvesting olives for oil. <br />
[ <a href="http://www.cnn.com/2002/WORLD/meast/08/29/mideast/index.html"><span class="caps">CNN</span></a> &#8211; <a href=http://story.news.yahoo.com/news?tmpl=story2&u=/020829/161/24zzj.html&e=3&ncid=705"> Photo</a> &#8211; <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2002/08/29/international/29CND-MIDE.html">NY Times</a> &#8211; <a href="http://www.haaretzdaily.com/hasen/pages/ShArt.jhtml?itemNo=202956&contrassID=1&subContrassID=0&sbSubContrassID=0">Ha&#8217;aretz</a> ]</p>
<p>It&#8217;s one of the top stories on the newswires right now, but the NY Times chose to put it on page 9 with a standard-sized headline.</p>
<p>The settlements are being built in areas where these families have lived for generations.  To kill them for being in the olive groves that they&#8217;ve tended for hundreds of years (or more) is terrorism.</p>
