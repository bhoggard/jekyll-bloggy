<p>The folks at <a href="http://www.complacent.org/">complacent.org</a> have set up a web site for people organizing against the evil GOP&#8217;s use of <span class="caps">NYC</span> and 9/11 for its own purposes: <a href="http://www.counterconvention.org/">Counter Convention</a>.</p>
<blockquote>From August 30th through September 4th, the Republicans will be invading New York City to nominate George W. Bush as their candidate for President. Never has a convention been hosted so late in the year and this is the first time a Republican Convention has been in New York City. Why host a convention in September? Why New York? By exploiting our grief and trauma from September 11th, the right wing intends to further their regressive political agenda.</blockquote>
