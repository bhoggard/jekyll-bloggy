<p>I went to the opening of &#8220;The (S) Files&#8221; at <a href="http://www.elmuseo.org/">El Museo Del Barrio</a>.  They have great opening parties &#8212; and the crowd, to quote <a href="http://www.uffish.com/archives/000927.html">Chris</a>, was Hhhhhhhottttt.</p>
<p>It&#8217;s definitely worth a visit &#8212; one of the best group shows I&#8217;ve seen lately.  Favorites: Alejandro Cesarco&#8217;s film of people playing scrabble, spelling out his influences (from Baudrillard to Leonard Cohen), <a href="http://amorphicrobotworks.org/people_chico.html">Chico MacMurtrie</a>&#8216;s big robotic monkey, and of course Maria Alos&#8217;s Manhattan project, since James and I were in the group of models:</p>
