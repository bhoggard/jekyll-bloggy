<blockquote>Miami police announced Monday they will stage random shows of force at hotels, banks and other public places to keep terrorists guessing and remind people to be vigilant.<br />
<br />
Deputy Police Chief Frank Fernandez said officers might, for example, surround a bank building, check the IDs of everyone going in and out and hand out leaflets about terror threats.<br />
<br />
&#8220;This is an in-your-face type of strategy. It&#8217;s letting the terrorists know we are out there,&#8221; Fernandez said.</blockquote>
