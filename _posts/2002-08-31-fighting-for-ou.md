<p>Supposedly we&#8217;re at war to &#8220;protect our culture&#8221;.  I&#8217;m fascinated by a country that gives us a <a href="http://www.staples.com/Catalog/Browse/department.asp?PageType=4&DepartmentID=1621&bcFlag=True&bcSCatId=1&bcSCatName=Office+Supplies&bcCatId=8&bcCatName=Maintenance+%26+Breakroom">Food and Beverage Category</a> in the Staples catalog.  When I worked at a dot-com, we knew things were headed downhill when the free food and drinks stopped.  My favorite excuse from the evil <span class="caps">CFO</span> was that &#8220;people might just eat junk here rather than go out and buy lunch.&#8221;  The idea that people working 12-18 hour days might save time by eating bad junkfood inside seemed horrible to him.  Maybe he just cared about my health.</p>
<p>I thought about posting the picture of Britney and Jacko at the <span class="caps">MTV</span> awards as another example of American culture, but that&#8217;s too easy.</p>
