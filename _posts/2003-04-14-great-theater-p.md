<p>Worth Street Theater, the closest theater to Ground Zero, presents <a href="http://www.worthstreettheaterco.org/voices.html">Voices of Peace &amp; Dissent from Ground Zero</a>.</p>
<blockquote>Beginning Monday, April 7, The Worth Street Theater Compapny @ The Tribeca Playhouse presents Voices of Peace &amp; Dissent from Ground Zero.<br />
<br />
Voices of Peace &amp; Dissent from Ground Zero will feature a rotating cast of actors, activists and celebrities in curated evenings of pro-peace/anti-war texts spanning history from the Ancient Greeks to the present day &#8211; featuring such political oratory as Robert Byrd&#8217;s speech to the Senate, Robin Cook&#8217;s recent resignation from the British Cabinet, as well as contributions from Eric Bogosian, Tony Kushner and other contemporary playwrights.</blockquote>
