<p>While Americans were watching footage of missiles and files on <span class="caps">CNN</span>, the House <a href="http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/articles/A1288-2003Mar20.html">passed</a> (215-212) the Bush budget which includes a $726 billion tax cut and an estimated deficit of at least  $350 billion.  Note that the deficit number does <b>not</b> include any estimate of the costs of the Iraq war.  The White House is expected to submit a request next week for &#8220;emergency spending&#8221; of about $80 billion.</p>
