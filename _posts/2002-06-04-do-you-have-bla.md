<p>Tom Tomorrow&#8217;s blog has a <a href="http://www.thismodernworld.com/weblog/archive/2002_05_26_bloggera.html#77079321">story</a> on Bush&#8217;s visit with Brazil&#8217;s President, in which he asked, &#8220;Do you have blacks too?&#8221;</p>
<p>The original <a href="http://www.spiegel.de/panorama/0,1518,196865,00.html">story</a> is in Der Spiegel.  I&#8217;m not surprised this one didn&#8217;t make it into the U.S. papers.</p>
<p>I suppose this could be an urban legend, but the point of the story is that with Bush we&#8217;re not sure.  I don&#8217;t think we could imagine Clinton or Carter or even G. Bush I asking such a thing.  With Bush II, it seems possible.</p>
