<p>Too tired to post much, but the galleries that made the biggest impression on me were:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Black &amp; White &#8211; Julian Montague and Kim Bennett</li>
	<li>31 Grand &#8211; the whole thing, but especially John Copeland, Tom Sanford, and Michael Cambre</li>
	<li>Andrew Edlin &#8211; &#8220;outsider&#8221; art of great interest, especially Tom Duncan</li>
	<li>Samson Projects</li>
	<li>Gallery Joe</li>
</ul>
<p>That&#8217;s certainly not the whole list, and Gae Savannah&#8217;s work at Curcio Projects and the entire room of Stephanie Theodore should not be missed either.  Overall I think it&#8217;s the strongest Scope I&#8217;ve seen.</p>
