<p>Rueben Cox, <em>Scaly Mountain, NC</em>, 2004, C-print</p>
<p>Thank goodness for Roberta Smith.  I probably would not have heard of this show without her <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2005/01/27/arts/design/27chan.html?oref=login">review</a> in today&#8217;s <em>New York Times</em>.  My favorite part (which refers to the image above):</p>
<blockquote>
<p>Exhibit A is the flame-haired belle in the flowered dress, pastel boa and extravagantly painted lips who has flung herself across a patch of green cow pasture as if it were the seamless paper in Richard Avedon&#8217;s studio. A half dozen oblivious cows grace the horizon.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Rueben Cox photographed drag and transgendered subjects in New York, New  Orleans, Memphis, and Nashville in 2004.  I have to try to get there before it closes on Saturday.  It&#8217;s on 17th Street in the space once occupied by <strike>Rupert Goldsworthy</strike> Marcus Ritter.</p>
