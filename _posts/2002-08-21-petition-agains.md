<p>As someone who thinks a pre-emptive strike on a country that few people other than Bush believe is a significant threat to us, I just signed Move On&#8217;s <a href="http://www.moveon.org/nowar/">petition</a>.  This group got started during the horrors of the Clinton impeachment/Republican coup.</p>
<p>I will not feel safe in a world, as an American and a New Yorker, that justifiably believes that force is the only way to convince the <span class="caps">USA</span> of anything.</p>
<p>A Republican administration that has Kissinger telling it this is a bad idea is a danger to us all.</p>
<p>P.S.  Are we really going to be safer if every country with weapons of mass destruction thinks we might attack them?  It brings new meaning to the phrase &#8220;use &#8217;em or lose &#8217;em&#8221;.</p>
