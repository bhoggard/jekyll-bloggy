<p>Wow.  We&#8217;re not going to be able to afford anything but cops in <span class="caps">NYC</span> after this week.  There are 3-6 cops per block on 9th Avenue from 14th to 23rd Streets, and at least 10 per block on 7th and 8th Avenues in the same area.</p>
<p>Big (and I mean that in several ways) presence of <span class="caps">NYPD</span> &#8220;guarding&#8221; Billy&#8217;s Bakery on 9th Avenue and the Maritime Hotel.  Maybe they&#8217;re looking for some <a href="http://www.gawker.com/topic/more-fans-of-the-maritime-hotel-015713.php">Upper East Side hotties</a>.</p>
<p>Orange cones everywhere.  The &#8220;Free Speech Zone&#8221; running north on 8th Avenue above 23rd Street is empty, and you have to get permission to even enter the sidewalks in that area.  We&#8217;re also seeing cops in plain clothes riding scooters around.  It&#8217;s hard to tell the non-badged thugs from the thugs, since they aren&#8217;t showing badges.</p>
<p>We have less freedom of movement at the moment than we did downtown in the days after 9/11.  See our <a href="http://jameswagner.com/ids/index.cgi?mode=album&album=/NYC/WTC">photos</a> from then for proof.</p>
