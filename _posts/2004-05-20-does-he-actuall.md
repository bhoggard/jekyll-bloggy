<blockquote>Kerry told The Associated Press on Wednesday that he would consider a judicial candidate who disagrees with his support of abortion rights as long as it doesn&#8217;t lead to the Supreme Court overturning Roe v. Wade, the landmark 1973 ruling that made abortion legal.<br />
<br />
Hours later, as fellow Democrats and abortion-rights supporters sought clarification, Kerry issued a statement pledging not to appoint anyone to the Supreme Court who would undo abortion rights. He left open the possibility of appointing anti-abortion judges to lower courts.</blockquote>
