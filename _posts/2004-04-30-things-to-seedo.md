<p>Go see <a href="http://www.dtw.org/dy_eventDetails.cfm?ID=16">SplitStream</a> (April 30-May 1 at 7pm) at Dance Theater Workshop.  It includes Ann Liv Young, of whom I&#8217;ve <a href="/2003/06/fresh-tracks-at.html">written before</a>, Jonathan Berger, and Antonio Ramos.  File under the theater side of &#8220;dance theater&#8221;, with a heavy emphasis on <a href="http://www.geocities.com/~mikehartmann/wildatheart/wahtime.html">&#8216;&#8217;Lordy, what was that all about?&#8217;&#8217;</a>  We loved it.</p>
<p>Go to <a href="http://www.momentaart.org/">Momenta</a>&#8216;s benefit, held at White Columns, on Saturday, May 1.  It&#8217;s one of the great art bargains in the city &#8211; a raffle of great art works for a $175 ticket.  We&#8217;ll be there, so don&#8217;t pick any of our favorites if your number gets drawn sooner than ours.</p>
<p>Go see <a href="/2004/04/joe-ovelman-the.html">Joe Ovelman</a> at Oliver Kamm, closing tomorrow.  Check out the <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2004/04/30/arts/design/30GALL.html?pagewanted=2&ei=5062&en=a4972718bacbc4f8&ex=1083988800&partner=GOOGLE">review</a> by Holland Cotter in today&#8217;s NY Times.  It sounds like he&#8217;s wondering what Joe&#8217;s up to, but whatever it is, he likes it.</p>
