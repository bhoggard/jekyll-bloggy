<p>Universities are much more cool these days.  There is an open wireless network here at the <a href="http://www.tum.de/">site</a> of the conference, so that I can use my notebook anywhere to connect.</p>
<p>At breakfast there was guy sitting at the other end of our table who I was convinced was an American geek headed for the same conference &#8212; he was wearing a Basics t-shirt after all.  He turned out to be a French journalist here to cover a big motorcycle show.  We apologized for thinking he might be American.</p>
