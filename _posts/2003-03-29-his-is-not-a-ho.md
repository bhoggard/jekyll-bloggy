<p>The title of this post, along with &#8220;We can&#8217;t hear your car alarms / from our renovated barn&#8221; are some of my favorite lyrics from <a href="http://www.peoplearewrong.net/">People Are Wrong</a>, a rock musical at <a href="http://www.ps122.org/">P.S.122</a>.  There are only two more performances &#8212; Saturday at 9pm and Sunday at 5pm.</p>
<p>Don&#8217;t just stand there!  Go <a href="http://www.ticketweb.com/user/nyc/venues/misc/ps122">buy tickets</a>!</p>
<p>The talented cast includes our friend <a href="http://www.driverquartet.com/">David Driver</a> (web site seems to be down), John Flansburgh from They Might Be Giants, and is directed by our friend <a href="http://www.targetmargin.org/">David Herskovitz</a>.  The cast also includes Maggie Moore from Hedwig, and the great music is written by <a href="http://www.cropduster.com/juliagreenberg/default.htm">Julia Greenberg</a> and Robin Goldwasser.</p>
