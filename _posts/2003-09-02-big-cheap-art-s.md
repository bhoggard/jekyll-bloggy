<p><a href="http://www.artretran.com/">Art Resources Transfer</a> is having a benefit on Thursday, September 4, from 7-9pm.  Artworks, valued from $250 on up, will be on sale for $100-$250.</p>
<p>They don&#8217;t seem to have updated the web site with this info, but head on over.  We first heard of some of our favorite artists &#8212; including <a href="/2003/02/joe-ovelman-dan.html">Joe Ovelman</a> and <a href="http://www.charlesgoldmanwork.com/">Charles Goldman</a> &#8212; through <span class="caps">ART</span>.</p>
<p>This is your last chance for one of these.  <span class="caps">ART</span> is ending its gallery program to concentrate on publishing and the <a href="http://www.ducprogram.org/"><span class="caps">DUC</span></a> (Distribution to Underserved Communities) program.</p>
