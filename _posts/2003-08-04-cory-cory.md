<p>I finally met <a href="http://www.beigerecords.com/cory/">Cory Arcangel</a> and heard him perform with his sister Jamie.  We got to see from home videos from their childhood, when they had a goth-ish/death metal band called &#8220;Insectiside&#8221;.  They did two songs live &#8212; very cool stuff.  Another highlight was a video of Jamie as a child, complete with knee pads and other 80s accoutrements, dancing to <a href="http://www.vanillaice.com/">Vanilla Ice</a>&#8217;s, &#8220;Ice Ice Baby&#8221;.  There!  Now you have that damn song running through your head too!</p>
