<p>Via <a href="http://eyeteeth.blogspot.com/2008/05/stake-claim-on-democracy-in-yer-yard.html">Eyeteeth</a> I learned about the <a href="http://myyardourmessage.com/">My Yard Our Message</a> project in honor of the Republican National Convention in Minneapolis.</p>
<blockquote>
<p>As a stark counterpoint to the &#8220;scripted democracy&#8221; of this fall&#8217;s <span class="caps">GOP</span> nominating convention, a project by mnartists.org, the Walker Art Center and the UnConvention is inviting people of all political stripes and artistic abilities to create yard signs to coincide with the <span class="caps">RNC</span> &#8212; and, fittingly, the 50 designs getting the most online votes will be produced and distributed around the Twin Cities and near the convention site.</p>
</blockquote>
