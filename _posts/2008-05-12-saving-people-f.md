<p>James and I were very proud to have Susan C. Dessel&#8217;s work in the <a href="http://danglingbetween.com/">show</a> we curated in 2006 at Dam, Stuhltrager.  Now that work has had a second chance to be seen, at the Long Beach Island Foundation for Arts &amp; Sciences, but the people that run it have chosen to put walls around it and warn people that it may &#8220;offend.&#8221;  See James&#8217;s <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/007057.html">post</a> for more information.</p>
<p>People don&#8217;t seem to be too outraged that torture is committed in our name by our elected government, but they can certainly be upset by an artwork that might remind them of a world that&#8217;s not as perfect as they would like to pretend.</p>
