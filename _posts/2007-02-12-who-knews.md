<p>Who knew that Chelsea had a pretty good symphony?  James and I attended a concert of the <a href="http://www.chelseasymphony.org/">Chelsea Symphony</a> last night and really enjoyed it.  The program consisted of a world premiere(!) of a work by Francesco Lecce-Chong, the Tchaikovsky Violin Concerto, and Brahms&#8217;s Symphony No. 3.  We didn&#8217;t know what to expect, but the performances were very good, and it&#8217;s wonderful to be able to attend a concert a block and a half from our apartment!  I also really appreciate the fact that the members play several roles &#8212; the composer is a violist, and the two conductors (one for each half of the program) were in the orchestra when they weren&#8217;t conducting.</p>
<p>The poster for the current show, and the program, feature a detail of a painting by our good friend and neighbor <a href="http://jameswagner.com/mt_archives/006042.html">Louise Fishman</a>.</p>
