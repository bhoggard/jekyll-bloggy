<p>When I first started ArtCal in 2004, ArtCat web hosting for artists <a href="/2003/08/meredith-allen-1.html">already existed</a>.  The latter doesn&#8217;t stand for anything, but ArtCal was suggested by a friend as short for &#8220;Art Calendar.&#8221;</p>
<p>Given the fact that I have trademarked <a href="http://www.artcat.com/">ArtCat</a>, and I own the .com for it, I always meant to consolidate everything &#8212; <a href="http://hosting.artcat.com/">artist and gallery web hosting</a>, <a href="http://calendar.artcat.com/home">the calendar</a>, and <a href="http://zine.artcat.com/">the zine</a>, into one brand with interlinked sites, and now I have!  As part of a redesign for the new <a href="http://beta.culturepundits.com/">Culture Pundits</a> advertising format, I worked with the brilliant artist and designer <a href="http://subsidiarydesign.com/">Michael Mandiberg</a> to come up with a new look.</p>
<p>We are still tweaking the design, but go <a href="http://www.artcat.com/">check it out</a>.  I&#8217;m quite happy with it.</p>
