<p>Do not miss David Humphrey&#8217;s <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/678">installation</a> at Morsel, up through June 5th, titled <em>Oven Stuffer Roaster</em>.  The title comes from the fact that he created these works using those turkey balloons with pilgrim hats one sees around Thanksgiving in the front yards of houses in neighborhoods such as Williamsburg.</p>
