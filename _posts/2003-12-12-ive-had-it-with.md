<p>The <a href="http://www.nps.gov/stli/">Statue of Liberty</a> is still closed, for &#8220;security reasons&#8221;, 27 months after 9/11.  It sends a nice message about our priorities, don&#8217;t you think?  There is a <a href="http://www.statueofliberty.org/">campaign</a> to raise private donations to re-open it, since our government can&#8217;t afford the $5 million to re-open it.  I just saw a <a href="https://bloggy.com/mt/archives/statue-liberty-banner.html" onclick="window.open('http://bloggy.com/mt/archives/statue-liberty-banner.html','popup','width=728,height=90,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0'); return false">banner ad</a> on My Yahoo!</p>
<p>$87 billion for Iraq, $5 million for the Statue of Liberty.  Think about it.  I guess President Flight Suit&#8217;s handlers don&#8217;t want a photo op in front of a symbol that might make people think of France or immigrants.</p>
