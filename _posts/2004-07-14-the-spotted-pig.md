<p>We had lunch with friends at <a href="http://thespottedpig.com/">The Spotted Pig</a> yesterday.</p>
<p>The gnudi (like gnocchi but only made of ricotta) with butter and sage: excellent.</p>
<p>The halibut with chunky puree of peas and sauteed escarole: yummy.</p>
<p>The service: <span class="caps">HORRIBLE</span>.</p>
<p>If you want to learn more about gnudi, this Rogers and Gray <a href="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/140005348X/tristanmedial-20">cookbook</a> (they of River Cafe in London fame) has a whole chapter.</p>
