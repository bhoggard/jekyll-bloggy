<p>Culture reminder: The <a href="http://www.museummilefestival.org/">Museum Mile Festival</a> is today from 6-9.  All of the museums from 82nd Street to 104th Street along Fifth Avenue have free admission, plus the avenue itself is closed to traffic and has bands, entertainment, etc.</p>
