title: "War, what is it good for?"
date: 2004-09-03 13:32:45 -04:00
categories: [War]
permalink: /2004/09/war-what-is-it.html
---

<p>As I read about the conventions and the presidential campaign, I was struck by something.  We have reached the point where the debate seems to be how to use the military to achieve our goals.  Even Prussia <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frederick_the_Great">at its most warlike</a> understood that the military was only one part of effective statecraft.</p>
<p>Are we really saying the only tool that this diverse, rich, and creative country has is war?</p>
<p>Our corporations are capable of putting refrigerated Coke and Disney products into some pretty remote parts of the world.  It&#8217;s a shame that we can&#8217;t put any ideas other than our ability to threaten people out there as well.</p>
