<p>More than one in five of the jobs lost nationwide over the past year <a href="http://www.crainsny.com/news.cms?newsId=5074">were in New York City</a>, according to the Center for an Urban Future, which says that the city accounts for only 2.8% of all jobs in the country.</p>
