<p>&#8230; remind yourself that Cindy Sheehan managed to visit 21 states and over 50 cities and towns before being threatened with arrest.  She had to come to <span class="caps">NYC</span> for that.</p>
<p>Tom Moody <a href="http://www.digitalmediatree.com/tommoody/?33139">has more</a>.  Allegedly it was over the use of amplification, but the city wouldn&#8217;t give them permits after they were requested, and Union Square can be a noisy place.</p>
<p>They should have added some religious content.  People like the &#8220;Black Israelites&#8221; in Times Square are allowed to yell anti-white and anti-gay crap and Holocaust denials, using bullhorns, under the courts&#8217; view of the First Amendment.  It&#8217;s interesting that the denial of permits by the <span class="caps">NYPD</span> for things like a Cindy Sheehan visit don&#8217;t seem to cause the same concern.</p>
