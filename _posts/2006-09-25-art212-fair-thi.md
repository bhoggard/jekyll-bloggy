<p>I&#8217;m surprised that I haven&#8217;t really seen mentions on art blogs of the new art fair, <a href="http://art-212.com/"><acronym title="212"><span class="caps">ART</span></acronym></a>, which opens this week.  I like the fact that the opening night <a href="http://art-212.com/preview.asp">preview</a> is a benefit for <a href="http://www.aidforaids.org/">Aid For <span class="caps">AIDS</span></a> and <a href="http://www.nyfa.org/">The New York Foundation For The Arts</a>, rather than for something like a big museum&#8217;s acquisition fund.</p>
<p>The private preview is Wednesday, September 27, 6-9PM.  Tickets are $75 each, or $130 for two.  After that it&#8217;s $15/day, or free on Friday from 6-9PM.</p>
<p>Some bloggy friends such as Daniel Cooney Fine Art, Moti Hasson Gallery, Hudson Franklin, Magnan Projects, Mixed Greens, Morgan Lehman, and Priska C. Juschka Fine Art are participating.  I know that Robert Koch (San Francisco) will have some <a href="http://www.notifbutwhen.com/">Brian Ulrich</a> photos available, for his <a href="http://modernartobsession.blogs.com/modern_art_obsession/2006/09/an_art_show_by_.html">fans</a> here.</p>
<p>There are also special projects, including an exhibition curated by the Asia Society and El Museo Del Barrio.</p>
