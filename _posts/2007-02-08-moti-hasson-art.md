<p><a href="http://www.motihasson.com/">Moti Hasson</a> has a great new ground floor space in Chelsea, with an excellent group show, titled <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/3890">&#8220;Beyond the Pale&#8221;</a>, as its inaugural exhibition.  Now I see Moti&#8217;s smiling face on the cover of <em>Chelsea Now</em>.  I like the fact that he started as a collector and decided to run a gallery, sort of what I could imagine us doing if we didn&#8217;t have to worry about New York real estate prices.</p>
