<p>You only have a week on two of these:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.blackandwhiteartgallery.com/e_mailer/invite_critspix.htm">crits&#8217;s pix</a> at Black &amp; White has great stuff, especially Julian Montague&#8217;s &#8220;The Stray Shopping Cart: An Illustrated System of Identification&#8221; and Jon-Paul Villegas&#8217;s brilliant mix of wall paintings and sculpture.  I&#8217;ll add images if I can get some.  Closes 7/19.</p>
<p>&#8220;a dot that went for a walk&#8221; at <a href="http://www.plusultragallery.com/">Plus Ultra Gallery</a> includes Katinka Ahlbom (who had a striking installation that was part of &#8220;Sunrise Sunset&#8221; at <a href="http://www.smackmellon.org/">Smack Mellon</a>), Vanessa Conte, Rosemarie Fiore, and Medrie Macphee. Closes 7/19.</p>
<p>We will have to go back to &#8220;Grotto 2&#8221; at <a href="http://www.jessicamurrayprojects.com/">Jessica Murray Projects</a>, as it was very hot and difficult to absorb the 60+ artists in the show.  A few things did manage to stand out, such as Rachel Mason&#8217;s video &#8220;Model Anthem&#8221;, the White House sculpture of Jesse Bercowetz and Matt Bua (we bought the &#8220;Todo List&#8221; work that went along with it), and Diane Meyer&#8217;s &#8220;Redemption: Professional Confessional.&#8221;  Some lucky person at the opening bought a Reed Anderson work based on a page from a 1977 Penthouse at a very reasonable price.  Closes 8/1.</p>
