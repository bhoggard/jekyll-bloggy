<p>Is he insane?  I can&#8217;t imagine why any British PM would tie his fortunes so closely to the most ignorant man ever to be President of the <span class="caps">USA</span>.</p>
<p>For him to <a href="http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/politics/2227345.stm">argue this</a>:</p>
<blockquote>The world cannot stand by while Iraq is in &#8220;flagrant breach&#8221; of United Nations resolutions, the UK prime minister has said.</blockquote>
<p>seems dangerous to me, given that one of the key &#8220;allies&#8221; of the US and UK in the Middle East is Israel, which is certainly <a href="http://www.jewsagainsttheoccupation.org/UNresolutions.html">in violation of a number of UN resolutions</a>.  It only makes the two countries look like hypocrites.</p>
<p>I&#8217;m not arguing that Iraq is country of peace and enlightenment, but I also don&#8217;t see it as a country that threatens us enough to spend $50+ billion (more than the federal government&#8217;s annual spending on primary and secondary education plus medical research) and risk thousands of American lives, to say nothing of de-stablizing much of the Middle East.</p>
