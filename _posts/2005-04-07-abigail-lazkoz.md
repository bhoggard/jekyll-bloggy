<p>I feel like such a bad print journalist when I write about shows right before they close (in the case of Abigail Lazkoz), but I wanted to mention these two drawing shows that really struck me.</p>
<p>&#8220;Struck me&#8221; is an interesting phrase to use, given the reason for this post.  Both of these shows are drawing shows with some admirable subtlety.  A lot of drawing shows today seem to be aimed at showing the kind of work that will stand out at big art fairs.  That&#8217;s not to say that a lot of it isn&#8217;t still worthwhile, but to find a couple of shows with excellent drawing that don&#8217;t have that kind of improvisatory, &#8220;look at me&#8221; quality is refreshing.  These are thought out, well-executed drawing shows not to be missed.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.monyarowegallery.com/lazkoz/index.html">Laskoz</a> is up through this Saturday, and <a href="http://www.jessicamurrayprojects.com/prevSherExhImages2.html&#39;s">Vicki Sher</a> show closes on the 19th.</p>
