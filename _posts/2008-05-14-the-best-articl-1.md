<p>Leave it to <em>Chelsea Now</em> to write the best <a href="http://chelseanow.com/cn_85/clementine.html">article</a> I&#8217;ve read on the closing of the beloved <a href="http://www.clementine-gallery.com/">Clementine Gallery</a>.  Maybe the art press is too afraid to dwell on such stories.  My favorite statistic in the article: the gallery needed to sell $80,000 worth of work each month to break even.</p>
<p>I chose the image above, from Farber&#8217;s 2004 show at the gallery, in honor of our buying several of his works from the gallery in the 90s.  I always loved sending people who were just starting out as art collectors to Clementine, as I knew Abby and Liz would treat them well.</p>
